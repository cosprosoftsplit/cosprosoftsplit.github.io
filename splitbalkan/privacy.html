SplitBalkan Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 21, 2026

SplitBalkan is a local-first group expense calculator. It calculates who owes whom; it does not move money, process payments, link bank accounts, or send funds.

Data Collection

SplitBalkan does not collect analytics, tracking identifiers, contact data, location data, photos, bank data, or payment account data. There is no account system and no backend service.

Local Data

Trips, participants, expense entries, currency settings, and exports stay on your device unless you choose to share a CSV or text summary using iOS sharing features.

Purchases

Premium unlocks are handled by Apple's StoreKit. SplitBalkan does not receive or store credit card numbers or payment credentials.

Contact

For privacy questions, email support@cosmicproduction.hr.
