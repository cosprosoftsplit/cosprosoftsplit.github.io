Offline group expenses

SplitBalkan

A local-first bill splitter for Balkan trips, shared stays, weekend groups, and multi-currency expenses.

What it does

Add trips, participants, and shared expenses.

Split evenly, by shares, by percentage, or by exact amounts.

Use bundled reference rates with manual overrides.

Export CSV records with the lifetime Premium unlock.

What it does not do

SplitBalkan calculates who owes whom. It does not connect to banks, initiate transfers, verify outside settlement, fetch current FX data, run ads, or use analytics SDKs.
