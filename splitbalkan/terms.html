SplitBalkan Terms

Last updated: May 21, 2026

Utility Only

SplitBalkan is provided as a utility for calculating shared expenses. It is not a bank, wallet, remittance service, accounting service, or financial advisor.

No Money Movement

The app calculates balances and settlement suggestions only. It does not initiate transfers, process payments, connect to banks, or verify whether any real-world payment occurred.

Reference Rates

Currency conversion uses a bundled reference-rate snapshot and optional user overrides. Rates may differ from actual exchange rates, bank rates, or card rates.

Premium

Premium is a lifetime non-consumable purchase handled by Apple's StoreKit. Apple terms and policies apply to purchase handling.
