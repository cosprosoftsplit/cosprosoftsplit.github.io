Frequently Asked Questions

Does SplitBalkan work offline?

Yes. Trips, expenses, bundled reference rates, and manual rate overrides work locally on device.

Can SplitBalkan send money?

No. SplitBalkan calculates balances and settlement suggestions only. Any real-world settlement happens outside the app.

Does SplitBalkan connect to bank or wallet accounts?

No. There is no bank linking, wallet linking, payment account collection, or payment initiation.

Does SplitBalkan use live exchange rates?

No. v1 uses a bundled reference-rate snapshot and user-editable overrides.
