TawlaAR — Terms

TawlaAR is a casual Tawla / backgammon learning and play app. It is not a competitive ruleset authority and does not claim to represent any federation or regional rulebook.

No real-money play. No virtual currency. No rewards table. The doubling cube is off by default.

Rules vary by region. v1 of TawlaAR uses a standard ruleset. Regional variant packs may be added in future versions after review by local players.

The bundled Arabic terminology in v1 is a draft pending review by a regional Tawla player.
