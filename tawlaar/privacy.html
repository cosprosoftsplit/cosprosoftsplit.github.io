TawlaAR — Privacy

TawlaAR collects no personal data. Game progress and settings stay on this device.

The camera is used only when you tap View in AR, to render the board on a surface in your room. Camera frames are not saved, shared, or sent anywhere. AR remains opt-in; the 2D experience does not require camera access.

No analytics. No ads. No account. No online multiplayer. No chat. No leaderboards.

You can wipe all local data from Settings → Delete all local data.
