ScanGenius Privacy Notice

ScanGenius scans documents on this device. Scanned images, OCR text, folders, tags, and notes stay on this device unless you explicitly export or share them via the system share sheet.

Data we do not collect

  • We do not run any analytics SDK.
  • We do not send your scans, OCR text, or document metadata to any server.
  • We do not use third-party advertising SDKs in this version.
  • We do not collect contacts, location, microphone, calendar, health, or background-refresh data.

Permissions we ask for

  • Camera: only when you tap Scan with camera, to capture document pages.
  • Photo Library (read): only when you tap Import from Photos.
  • Photo Library (add): only when you tap Save to Photos for an exported image.

OCR

OCR runs on this device via Apple's Vision framework. Recognized text is stored locally with your document. OCR is best effort and may contain errors — check important text before relying on it.

Sharing

Exporting a document via the system share sheet sends a copy to the destination you pick (Files, Mail, Messages, etc.). What happens to that copy is governed by that destination's own privacy notice.

Delete your data

Settings → Local data → Delete all local documents removes every document, page image, OCR result, and export record. Deleting the app from your device also removes any local data that has not been exported.

Children

ScanGenius is a general-purpose utility, not directed at children under 13.
