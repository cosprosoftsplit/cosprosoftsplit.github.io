Common questions

Why no camera prompt on first launch?

We only ask for camera or photo permission when you tap a button that needs it. That way you can browse settings, the inbox, and the empty state without granting access.

Does ScanGenius send my scans to a server?

No. Pages, OCR text, folders, tags, and notes stay on this device unless you explicitly export or share them via the system share sheet.

Why is some recognized text wrong?

OCR is best effort. Apple's on-device Vision framework does a good job most of the time but can mis-read unusual fonts, handwriting, or low-light scans. Re-scan or retype the parts that matter.

How do I unlock unlimited scans?

Settings → Premium → Unlock Premium. Premium is a one-time purchase and is restored via Settings → Premium → Restore purchases.

How do I delete everything?

Settings → Local data → Delete all local documents.
