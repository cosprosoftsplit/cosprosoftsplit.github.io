Support

Need help with SecurePass? The app runs entirely on your device, so there is no account to recover and no password we can reset for you.

If something does not work, try closing and reopening the app, or removing it and reinstalling from the App Store. The Settings screen offers a Clear all local data option that removes saved profiles, checklist progress, and App Lock preferences from this device.

For all other questions, email support@cosmicproduction.hr.
