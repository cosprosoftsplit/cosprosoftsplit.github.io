Privacy

SecurePass runs entirely on your device. We do not collect, transmit, sync, or store your real passwords. We do not maintain user accounts. We do not run analytics. We do not show ads.

The app generates passphrases locally using Apple's Security framework. It stores only non-sensitive items on this device: your saved generator profiles, your checklist progress, and your App Lock preference. You can clear all local data from Settings.

The strength coach analyses the pattern you type in-memory. It never sends the pattern off the device. The field clears automatically after a short period of inactivity and on backgrounding.

The generator's copy button writes the generated passphrase to the system pasteboard so you can paste it elsewhere. The pasteboard entry is cleared automatically after a short period and on backgrounding. SecurePass never reads the pasteboard.

SecurePass does not check breached-password databases. SecurePass does not provide autofill. SecurePass is not affiliated with any password manager.

StoreKit communicates with the App Store to verify any optional one-time purchase. No other network call is made.
