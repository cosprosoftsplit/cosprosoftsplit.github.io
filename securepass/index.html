SecurePass

SecurePass is a local passphrase generator and password-safety coach for iPhone and iPad.

It helps you generate readable passphrases, test sample patterns, and follow private account-safety checklists without an account.

  • No password vault or credential storage.
  • No account, analytics, ads, cloud sync, or third-party SDKs.
  • Generated passphrases stay on device and copy only when you tap Copy.
  • Checklist progress and custom generator profiles stay local.
