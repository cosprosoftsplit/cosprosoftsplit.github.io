TimeBudget

Private time blocking for planning hours, running focus sessions, and reviewing planned vs actual time each week.

What TimeBudget Does

  • Plans the day with simple time buckets like Deep Work, Admin, Study, Errands, Movement, and Recovery.
  • Runs local focus sessions and records honest actual time.
  • Shows a weekly planned-vs-actual review and supports Premium CSV export.

Privacy Posture

No accounts, no analytics, no ads, no cloud sync, no Calendar access in v1, and no third-party SDKs. Data stays on the device.
