Quick tips

  • Build a day fast. Open Today, tap "Apply Template", pick Workday or Study Day.
  • Run a focus session. Tap "Start Focus" on a bucket row. Use Pause to step away. Use Finish to log time. Use Abandon to stop without finishing (the elapsed time still counts toward actuals so the totals are honest).
  • Check the week. Weekly Review shows planned vs actual per bucket.

Reminders

Open Settings to toggle the planning, session-end, and review reminders. Notifications stay local on this device.

Delete data

Settings > Delete all local data. Double-confirm. This removes every bucket, plan, session, and template.
