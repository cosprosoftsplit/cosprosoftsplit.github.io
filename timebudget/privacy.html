Privacy Policy

Updated: 17 May 2026

Summary

TimeBudget is a private, offline time-blocking utility. Your buckets, plans, focus sessions, and notes are stored on this device. There is no account, no cloud sync, and no analytics in v1.

What stays on this device

  • Time buckets you create.
  • Daily plans and allocations.
  • Focus session records and any notes you add.
  • Settings and notification preferences.

What we do not collect

  • No contacts, location, microphone, camera, photos, health data, or Bluetooth.
  • No third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.
  • No team monitoring. No social ranking. No outside scoring of your hours.

Calendar

TimeBudget does not access your device calendar in this version. The Settings screen shows a placeholder for a possible future calendar import. If we ever ship that feature, it will be read-only, user-initiated, and clearly explained before the system prompt appears.

Notifications

TimeBudget can send local reminders for planning, focus session ends, and evening review. The notification permission is requested only when you turn on a reminder, not at first launch. All reminders stay on this device.

Purchases

If you buy Premium, the purchase is processed by Apple's StoreKit. We do not receive your name, email, or payment details.

Deleting data

Settings > Delete all local data removes every bucket, plan, allocation, session, note, template, and entitlement cache. You can do this at any time without uninstalling.
