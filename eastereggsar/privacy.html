Privacy Policy

Updated: 21 May 2026

Summary

EasterEggsAR runs entirely on your device. Hunt sessions, placed eggs, results, settings, and entitlement state stay local to the app.

What stays on this device

  • Hunt setup choices.
  • Placed eggs and board-hunt progress.
  • Results and settings.
  • Premium entitlement cache.

What we do not collect

  • No names, email addresses, accounts, family profiles, child names, child photos, or child locations.
  • No location, contacts, photos, microphone, health data, Bluetooth, or calendar data.
  • No cloud sync, shared anchors, multiplayer networking, public sharing, or leaderboards.
  • No third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

Camera

Camera access is requested only when you choose the optional AR Hunt flow. The board hunt works without camera access. Camera frames, AR world maps, and room scans are not saved or shared by the app.

Purchases

If you buy Premium, the purchase is processed by Apple's StoreKit. We do not receive your name, email, or payment details.

Deleting data

You can clear local hunt history from Settings in the app. Premium entitlement can be restored through StoreKit.
