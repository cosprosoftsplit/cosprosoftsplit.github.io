Updated: 21 May 2026

Getting started

Create a hunt, choose a play space, pick Board Hunt or optional AR Hunt, and then start play. Board Hunt works without camera permission.

Optional AR mode

AR Hunt uses the camera only after you choose the AR flow. If AR is unavailable or camera access is denied, use Board Hunt instead.

Spring Hunt copy

Open Settings and enable Spring Hunt copy for more general seasonal wording in schools and mixed households.

Purchases

Premium is a one-time lifetime purchase. Use Restore Purchases in Settings if Premium does not appear after reinstalling or changing devices.
