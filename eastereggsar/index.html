EasterEggsAR

EasterEggsAR is a private family egg hunt app for creating board hunts and optional AR hunts in a room, yard, classroom, or party space.

The board hunt works without camera permission. Optional AR mode asks for camera access only after you choose AR Hunt, and camera frames are not saved or shared.

The app has no account, no cloud sync, no analytics, no advertising SDK, no tracking, no multiplayer networking, no public sharing, and no leaderboards.

Premium is a one-time lifetime unlock for the Golden Hunt egg pack and Party Dash hunts with up to 12 eggs.
