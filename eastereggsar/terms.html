Use of the app

EasterEggsAR is a general-audience family activity for creating board hunts and optional AR egg hunts. Children should play with an adult nearby.

Physical safety

Use the app in a clear area, watch your steps, and avoid stairs, streets, fragile objects, or unsafe spaces while playing.

Premium purchase

Premium is a one-time, non-renewing unlock processed by Apple's StoreKit. It unlocks the Golden Hunt egg pack and Party Dash hunts with up to 12 eggs.
