BalootPro Terms

Effective date: May 20, 2026

BalootPro is an offline trainer and table companion for Baloot.

BalootPro is not a gambling app. There are no coins, bets, wagers, prizes, real-money play, online multiplayer, chat, public profiles, leaderboards, or ranked matchmaking.

Rules vary by region. BalootPro reflects common Gulf-region Saudi-variant rules and does not claim authoritative, federation, tournament, or official status.

Bundled Arabic terminology is a v1 draft pending review by a native Gulf-Arabic Baloot player before Arabic store metadata is published.
