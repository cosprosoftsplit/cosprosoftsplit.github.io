BalootPro Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 20, 2026

BalootPro does not collect personal data.

Scorekeeper history, drill progress, settings, and entitlement cache stay on the device. BalootPro has no accounts, analytics SDK, advertising SDK, telemetry, cloud sync, third-party network requests, Game Center, or tracking domains.

Deleting local data in Settings removes local scorekeeper history and drill progress.

For privacy questions, contact Cosmic Production d.o.o. at privacy@cosmicproduction.hr.
