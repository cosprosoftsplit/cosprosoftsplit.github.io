BalootPro

BalootPro is an offline Baloot trainer and pass-and-play table companion for Gulf-Arabic card players.

Learn common Saudi-region Baloot rules, practice bidding decisions, solve trick puzzles, play against transparent rule-based bots, and keep score for a physical-table game.

BalootPro has no accounts, analytics, advertising SDK, Game Center, chat, online multiplayer, coins, prizes, bets, wagers, or real-money play.

Important Scope

Rules vary by region. BalootPro reflects common Saudi-region defaults and does not claim official, federation, tournament, or authoritative status. Arabic terminology is reviewed before store-facing Arabic metadata is published.
