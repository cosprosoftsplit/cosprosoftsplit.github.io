Privacy Policy

Calculadora Autonomo 2026 — Last updated 2026-04-15

Introduction

Calculadora Autonomo 2026 is developed by Cosmic Production d.o.o. (“we”, “us”), based in Split, Croatia. This policy explains how the app handles data and which third-party services it uses.

Data We Collect Ourselves

None. All tax calculations are performed locally on your device. We do not run servers that store your income figures, expense inputs, calculation history, or any other personal data. The app does not contain analytics, crash reporting, or custom telemetry.

Data Transmitted to Third Parties

The app uses one third-party SDK that does transmit data: Google AdMob (advertising). This happens only in the free version; the in-app purchase removes ads and stops these transmissions for purchased users.

What AdMob transmits

Each time an ad loads, the AdMob SDK sends an ad request to Google’s servers. That request includes:

  • Technical device data: device model, operating system version, language, approximate IP-based location, screen size, app bundle identifier.
  • Advertising identifier (IDFA): transmitted only if you grant App Tracking Transparency (ATT) consent. If you deny ATT, the SDK falls back to non-personalised ads and the IDFA is not shared.
  • Ad interaction data: impressions, clicks, and basic engagement metrics for the ads displayed.

Google acts as the data controller for everything AdMob collects. See Google’s Privacy Policy and the AdMob data disclosure for full details.

Managing ATT consent

You can revoke or grant tracking consent at any time in iOS: Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking, then toggle Calculadora Autonomo 2026. Denying tracking does not disable ads; it only prevents the IDFA from being shared for personalised advertising.

Other Services

  • Apple App Store and StoreKit — used for app distribution and in-app purchases. Purchase receipts are handled by Apple; we never see your Apple ID, payment method, or card details. Apple’s Privacy Policy applies.
  • Apple iCloud — calculation history created by premium users is stored locally on-device via SwiftData. If you have enabled iCloud for this app in iOS Settings, Apple may replicate that data across your own devices. We do not have access to it.

Children’s Privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children. Ads served by AdMob are constrained by Google’s policies; the app does not opt into child-directed treatment because the target audience is self-employed professionals.

Your Rights Under GDPR

Because we do not collect or store personal data ourselves, there is no user-specific record for us to access, correct, or delete. For data processed by Google via the AdMob SDK, please exercise your rights directly through Google’s privacy controls.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy when the app’s data practices change (for example, adding a new third-party SDK). Material changes will be reflected in an updated “Last updated” date at the top.
