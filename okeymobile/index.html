OkeyMobile

An offline Turkish Okey trainer for learning rules, solving rack puzzles, practicing safer discards, playing against local bots, and keeping table scores on the same device.

Privacy Support Terms

Private Practice

OkeyMobile runs on device with no accounts, cloud sync, analytics, tracking, ads, or online multiplayer.

Trainer Tools

Study compact rule topics, solve rack arrangement puzzles, try discard drills, and review bilingual glossary entries.

Table Companion

Use the manual scorekeeper for physical-table rounds and export a plain-text summary when Premium is unlocked.

Review Posture

Rule and terminology content is marked as a draft pending native Turkish Okey player review. OkeyMobile does not offer real-money play, virtual currency, prizes, public rankings, Game Center, chat, or official tournament/federation authority.
