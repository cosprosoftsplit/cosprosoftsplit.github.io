OkeyMobile Privacy Policy

OkeyMobile is an offline Turkish Okey trainer and table companion.

Data Collection

OkeyMobile does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data. App Privacy in App Store Connect should be declared as Data Not Collected.

Local Storage

Scorekeeper history, practice progress, settings, and StoreKit entitlement state stay on the device. None of this data leaves the device.

Permissions

OkeyMobile does not request Contacts, Location, Microphone, Camera, Photos, Health, Bluetooth, Calendar, tracking, or advertising permissions.

Third Parties

OkeyMobile contains no third-party SDKs, analytics, advertising IDs, telemetry, tracking domains, or online game services. It uses Apple frameworks including SwiftUI, SwiftData, StoreKit 2, and Foundation.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact support@softsplit.com.
