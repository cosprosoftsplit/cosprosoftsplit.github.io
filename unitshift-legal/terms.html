UnitShift Terms

UnitShift converts units using bundled formulas. The conversions are provided "as is" without warranty. Verify any result you rely on for safety, regulatory, medical, or financial decisions against a primary source.

Accuracy

SI and imperial factors in the bundled catalog are taken from public definitions. "Month" and "year" are presented as calendar approximations. UnitShift does not provide fiat conversion rates in this version.

UnitShift Premium is a one-time, non-renewing purchase that unlocks unlimited favorites and unlimited history. Core conversions stay free.
