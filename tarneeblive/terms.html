TarneebLive Terms

Effective date: 2026-05-20

Purpose

TarneebLive is an offline trainer, bot-table practice app, and scorekeeper for Tarneeb. The app is for learning and table-companion use only.

No gambling

TarneebLive is not a gambling app. It has no bets, no prizes, no real-money play, no virtual currency, and no cash-out mechanism.

No online play

TarneebLive v1 is offline-only on a single device. It has no online multiplayer, chat, public profiles, leaderboards, or Game Center integration. The word "Live" is a brand name only.

Rules and review status

Tarneeb rules vary by region and table. TarneebLive reflects a common Levantine-style draft and does not claim authoritative, federation, legal, religious, school, tournament, or gambling status. Bundled Arabic terminology and rule content require native Levantine-Arabic Tarneeb player review before submission.

Purchases

Premium unlocks the shipped Premium features described in the app: full drill library access, unlimited undo during table practice, and scorekeeper plain-text export. Purchases are processed by Apple.
