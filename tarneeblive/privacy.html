TarneebLive Privacy

Effective date: 2026-05-20

Data collection

TarneebLive does not collect personal data. The app has no account sign-in, analytics SDK, ad SDK, telemetry, cloud sync, tracking SDK, or third-party network request flow.

Local data

Scorekeeper history, drill progress, Premium display cache, and settings stay on the device. The user can delete local scorekeeper and progress data in Settings.

Purchases

Premium purchases are handled by Apple through StoreKit. TarneebLive receives entitlement status from Apple so the app can unlock Premium features. SoftSplit does not receive payment card details or Apple account credentials.

Tracking and advertising

TarneebLive does not show ads and does not track users across apps or websites.

Contact

For support, use the support contact shown on the App Store listing.
