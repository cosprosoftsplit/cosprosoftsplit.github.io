CodeFlow Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 20, 2026

CodeFlow is an offline-first coding-concepts practice app. We do not operate a backend for the app, create user accounts, include analytics, or include advertising SDKs.

Data Collection

We do not collect personal data from CodeFlow. Progress, missed questions, streaks, review queue items, nickname, reminder settings, and purchase entitlement cache are stored locally on your device.

Purchases

Premium purchases are processed by Apple through StoreKit. We do not receive your payment card information.

Notifications

If you enable the optional daily lesson nudge, iOS asks for notification permission at that time. Reminder scheduling happens on your device.
