PortfolioPro Privacy Notice

PortfolioPro helps you draft portfolio case studies and resume bullet drafts on this device. Your projects, sections, metrics, tools, links, and resume bullet drafts stay on this device unless you explicitly export or share them via the system share sheet.

Data we do not collect

  • We do not run any analytics SDK.
  • We do not send your project content, metrics, or any other entered text to any server.
  • We do not use third-party advertising SDKs in this version.
  • We do not collect contacts, location, microphone, camera, photos, calendar, health, notifications, or background-refresh data.
  • We do not scrape LinkedIn, Indeed, Glassdoor, or any other site.
  • We do not provide hiring outcome guarantees, job placement, salary projections, interview preparation, ATS scoring, or AI resume review.

Permissions we ask for

  • None. PortfolioPro requests no permissions in this version.

Sharing

Exporting your case study draft via the system share sheet sends a copy of the text or markdown to the destination you pick (Mail, Messages, Files, etc.). What happens to that copy is governed by that destination's own privacy notice.

Delete your data

Settings → Clear local data removes every project, section, metric, tool, link, and resume bullet draft. Premium status is preserved. Deleting the app from your device also removes any local data that has not been exported.

Children

PortfolioPro is a general-purpose creative utility, not directed at children under 13.
