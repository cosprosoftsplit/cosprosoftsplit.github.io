PortfolioPro Terms of Use

PortfolioPro is a local case-study and resume bullet draft builder. It assembles structured copy from text you enter. It does not provide professional career advice, legal advice, financial advice, or any guaranteed hiring outcome.

What this app does

  • Helps you draft and organize portfolio case studies from project facts you enter.
  • Generates resume bullet drafts from your entered project facts (deterministic template-driven composer; no AI review).
  • Renders a markdown or plain-text preview that you can share with the destination of your choice.

What this app does not do

  • No hiring outcome, interview, salary, admissions, or career-change guarantees.
  • No ATS scoring or ATS optimization claims.
  • No AI resume review or AI portfolio review.
  • No backend file storage, cloud sync, or remote job board integration.
  • No scraping of any third-party job site or professional profile service.

Your responsibilities

  • Review every line of generated draft text before sharing.
  • Do not include confidential client information unless you have permission.
  • Metrics are optional. Specific context matters more than inflated numbers.
  • You are responsible for any assets you reference; the app stores only links and notes locally.

Premium

Premium is a one-time non-consumable unlock. Apple's standard refund policy applies. The free tier always remains usable for up to 3 active projects with starter templates.
