PortfolioPro Support

PortfolioPro is a local case-study and resume bullet draft builder. Everything stays on this device.

Getting started

  1. Open the Projects tab and tap New project.
  2. Pick a role template (Designer, Developer, or Marketer in the free tier).
  3. Open the Build tab and fill in the guided sections, metrics, tools, and links.
  4. Tap Preview case study to see the assembled draft.
  5. Tap Generate resume bullets to render three editable bullet drafts.
  6. Tap Export to share markdown or plain text via the system share sheet.

Frequently asked

Does PortfolioPro review my resume? No. The app composes drafts from facts you enter. There is no AI review, ATS scoring, or hiring outcome promise.

Where is my data? Only on this device. There is no cloud sync, no account, no backend, no analytics.

How do I delete everything? Settings → Clear local data wipes every project, section, metric, tool, link, and resume bullet draft. Premium status is preserved.

Can I add photos? Not in this version. Reference assets by link or note instead. Photo attachments may arrive in a future update with a clear permission gate.
