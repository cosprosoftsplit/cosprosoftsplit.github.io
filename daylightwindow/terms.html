What Daylight Window does

Daylight Window calculates sunrise, sunset, golden hour, solar noon, civil twilight, and daylight duration from latitude, longitude, and the current date using a native port of the public-domain NOAA Solar Position Algorithm. v1 does not include live weather, UV index, or air quality data.

What Daylight Window does NOT do

Daylight Window does not diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any condition. It is a planning utility only and makes no health, wellness, or treatment claims of any kind. It does not guarantee weather conditions or travel safety.
