Common questions

Does the app give legal advice?

No. EmploymentExitOS is an organizer and source index. It is not legal, tax, payroll, immigration, union, or employment advice.

Does the app calculate severance or redundancy pay?

No. The app does not compute statutory redundancy pay, Abfindung, finiquito, severance, or any other payout. When relevant, it links to separate sibling apps for estimates.

Does it store my documents?

No. v1 stores document-slot metadata only: title, filename hint, and note. The actual file stays wherever you keep it.

Does it send my data to a server?

No. Cases, dates, checklist items, questions, and document-slot metadata stay on this device unless you explicitly export a planning packet through the system share sheet.

How do I delete everything?

Open Settings and choose Delete all local data.
