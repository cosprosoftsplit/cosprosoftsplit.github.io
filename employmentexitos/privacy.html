EmploymentExitOS Privacy Notice

EmploymentExitOS stores your exit cases, dates, checklist items, questions, and document-slot metadata locally on this device.

Data we do not collect

  • We do not run analytics SDKs.
  • We do not use third-party advertising SDKs.
  • We do not send employer aliases, exit dates, questions, checklist items, or document-slot metadata to any server.
  • We do not collect contacts, location, camera, photo library, microphone, calendar, health, or background-refresh data.

Document slots

Document slots store metadata only: title, filename hint, and note. v1 does not store document file contents.

Sharing

Exporting a planning packet via the system share sheet sends a copy to the destination you pick. What happens to that copy is governed by that destination's own privacy notice.

Delete your data

Settings → Delete all local data removes every case, checklist item, timeline event, question, and document slot you created. Deleting the app from your device also removes local data that has not been exported.

Children

EmploymentExitOS is a general-purpose productivity utility, not directed at children under 13.
