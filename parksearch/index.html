ParkSearch

ParkSearch is an on-device parking memory, timer, saved-place, and return-to-car utility for iPhone and iPad.

The app stores your parking notes locally. It does not show live parking availability, process payments, verify legal parking, predict enforcement, or guarantee prices, hours, restrictions, or availability.

Location is requested only when you save a parked location or ask to show your location on the map. Notifications are requested only for parking timers. Photo access is requested only when you attach a parking photo.

Premium is a one-time lifetime unlock for unlimited saved parking places.
