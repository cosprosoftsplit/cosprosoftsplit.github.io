Common Questions

Does ParkSearch show open parking spaces?

No. ParkSearch does not show live availability or predict enforcement. It stores your own parking notes and timers.

Does ParkSearch process parking payments?

No. Payment notes are free-text reminders only. Use official signs, meters, city websites, garages, or payment apps as the source of truth.

Why does the return arrow move slowly?

The return arrow uses device location and compass data. Holding the phone away from magnets and large metal surfaces can improve heading updates.
