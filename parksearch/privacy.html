ParkSearch Privacy Policy

ParkSearch collects no personal data. Parking sessions, saved places, reminders, settings, and photo references stay on your device.

Location

ParkSearch asks for location only when you tap to save your current parked location or show your location on the map. Coordinates are used for your saved parking record and return-to-car view.

Notifications

ParkSearch asks for notification permission only when you set a parking timer. Notifications are scheduled locally by iOS.

Photos

If you attach a parking photo, ParkSearch stores the photo library asset identifier so it can show the selected photo later. The photo remains in your Photos library.

No Tracking

ParkSearch does not use analytics, advertising SDKs, tracking SDKs, accounts, cloud sync, or remote logging in v1.

Limitations

ParkSearch is a parking memory utility. It does not verify legal parking, process payments, predict enforcement, or guarantee prices, hours, restrictions, or availability.
