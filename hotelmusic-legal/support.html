Contact

For App Store review, billing escalations after Apple support, channel requests, commercial-use licensing letters, or bug reports, email support@meridianchapters.com.

We aim to reply within two business days in English, Croatian, or German.

Subscription help

Subscriptions are managed by Apple. To cancel or change a channel plan, open the Settings app on your device, tap your Apple ID, then tap Subscriptions. You can also use the Manage subscriptions button inside Meridian Chapters: Hotel Music.

Refunds are handled by Apple at reportaproblem.apple.com.

Venue licensing letters

If a hotel, restaurant, spa, bar, cafe, or other venue needs written confirmation that its Meridian Chapters channel subscription covers in-venue background playback, contact support with:

  • Venue legal name
  • Venue address
  • Active channel name
  • Apple subscription receipt or transaction reference if available

Troubleshooting

If a subscribed channel does not unlock, force-quit and reopen the app, then use Restore purchases in Settings. If playback does not start, confirm the device can reach play.meridianchapters.com over HTTPS and try a different network.
