Short version

Meridian Chapters: Hotel Music collects no personal data. We do not track you, do not profile you, do not sell or share your information, and do not use analytics, crash reporters, or advertising SDKs inside the app. The only data flows that exist are (a) Apple processing your subscription purchases, which we do not see, and (b) your device streaming music from our servers, which we do not log against your identity.

If that short answer is enough for you, you can stop reading. The rest is the formal version required by GDPR and Apple's App Review.

1. What we collect: nothing

We do not collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your phone number
  • Your location
  • Your device identifiers (IDFA, IDFV)
  • Your contacts
  • Your photos, videos, or audio
  • Your usage patterns, listening history, or channel preferences
  • Your IP address in any form linked to you personally
  • Your Apple ID or any account information
  • Any behavioral or advertising signal
  • Any biometric or health information

The App has no user account. You do not register. You do not log in. You simply open the App and press play.

2. What Apple collects (and we don't see)

When you subscribe to a channel, Apple processes the payment through your Apple ID. Apple provides us with:

  • An anonymous transaction ID tied to your subscription product
  • The fact that a valid subscription exists for a given product

We receive this information through Apple's StoreKit framework on your device, not through our servers. Apple's handling of your payment data is governed by Apple's own privacy policy.

3. Streaming data flows

When you press play, your device connects directly to our streaming servers at play.meridianchapters.com to receive audio. Like any internet connection, this exposes your device's IP address to our streaming server in standard HTTP logs.

What we do with those logs: we retain them for a maximum of 7 days solely for the purpose of detecting technical faults and abuse (for example, a single IP pulling streams 24/7 in violation of the Terms of Service). After 7 days, logs are automatically deleted.

What we do NOT do with those logs:

  • We do not link IP addresses to any user identity (we have no user identities)
  • We do not build profiles
  • We do not share them with advertisers
  • We do not enrich them with third-party data
  • We do not use them for analytics beyond server-capacity planning

Under GDPR, IP addresses are considered personal data. Our legal basis for this minimal 7-day retention is legitimate interest (Article 6(1)(f) GDPR) in keeping our service running and preventing abuse.

4. Channel catalog

The channel catalog (which channels are available, their descriptions, and their artwork) is bundled inside the App binary and does not require any network request. Unlike many apps, opening Meridian Chapters: Hotel Music does not phone home to any server — the only outbound network activity is the audio stream you explicitly start.

5. What we do NOT use

To make the "we collect nothing" commitment concrete, here is a list of common tools this App does not use:

  • Google Analytics / Firebase Analytics
  • Meta SDK / Facebook Pixel
  • Crashlytics / Bugsnag / Sentry
  • AppsFlyer / Adjust / Branch / Singular
  • TikTok SDK, Twitter SDK, any social login
  • Mixpanel, Amplitude, Segment, Hotjar
  • Any advertising SDK, including Google AdMob

The App uses only Apple's own frameworks (SwiftUI, AVFoundation, StoreKit 2) and connects only to one domain: play.meridianchapters.com (for audio streams).

6. Children

The App is intended for commercial use by adults representing hospitality venues. It is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children, because we do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone.

7. Your rights

Because we collect no personal data, there is effectively nothing for you to access, correct, delete, port, or object to. That said, you have the following rights under GDPR, CCPA, and similar laws:

  • Right of access — confirmation of what we hold. Answer: nothing tied to you.
  • Right to erasure — deletion of your data. Answer: there is nothing to delete.
  • Right to data portability — export of your data. Answer: there is nothing to export.
  • Right to object — to processing based on legitimate interest. The only such processing is the 7-day streaming log described in Section 3. You can object by writing to support@meridianchapters.com and we will add your IP range to a log-exclusion list within 30 days.
  • Right to lodge a complaint — with your national data protection authority. For Croatia, that is Agencija za zaštitu osobnih podataka (AZOP) at www.azop.hr.

10. Children in the EU

Under Article 8 GDPR, children under 16 (or the age set by their member state, down to 13) cannot consent to data processing. Since we collect no personal data, this provision does not affect the App. Payment processing, if a minor somehow accesses the App, is subject to Apple's family sharing and parental controls.
