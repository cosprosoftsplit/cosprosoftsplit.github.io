Roast Battle Party

Roast Battle Party is a clean local pass-and-play comedy card game for friends around one device.

The app uses fictional prompts, curated response cards, local voting, and one-tap skip. It has no account, no online multiplayer, no chat, no user-generated prompts, no analytics, no advertising, no tracking, and no third-party SDKs in v1.

Premium is a one-time lifetime unlock for the bundled premium preview decks: Friendly Chaos, Roommate Energy, and Stream Safe.
