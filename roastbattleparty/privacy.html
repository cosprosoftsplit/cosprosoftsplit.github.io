Privacy Policy

Roast Battle Party is a local party game. The app does not collect personal data, does not use analytics, does not include third-party trackers, and does not transmit any information off your device.

The information the app stores locally on your device is limited to:

  • Local player aliases (short pseudonyms you type at game setup)
  • Game history (which deck was played, who won)
  • Rule-acknowledgement timestamps
  • Premium-entitlement cache (whether you have unlocked the Premium IAP)

None of the above is uploaded, synced, or shared. You can clear all of it from the Settings screen, under "Delete all local data".

The app does not request access to contacts, photos, the camera, the microphone, location, or Bluetooth.

For privacy questions, contact support@cosmicproduction.hr.
