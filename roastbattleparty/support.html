Support

Need help? Have feedback on a card? Want to flag a tone problem?

Email us at support@cosmicproduction.hr. We read every message and use the feedback to improve the editorial review of the card packs.

If a specific card feels off-tone to your group, tap "Skip card" on the prompt screen — it is always one tap. If you want that card removed permanently, please tell us its prompt text (or screenshot) and we'll review it for the next content update.

This app does not include online play, chat, or any way to message other players inside the app. Everything happens at one table, on one device.
