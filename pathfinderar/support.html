PathFinderAR - Support

Need help? Email support@softsplit.app.

PathFinderAR is maintained by Cosmic Production d.o.o. for the SoftSplit app portfolio.

Common questions:

  • Why is the compass blank? PathFinderAR needs location access. Open Settings → Privacy → Location and enable When-in-use for PathFinderAR.
  • Why does AR say "Not supported"? AR requires an ARKit-capable device. The 2D compass and map have the full experience without AR.
  • Where is my data stored? On this device only. Nothing is uploaded.
  • Can I delete everything? Yes. Settings → Delete all local data (double-confirm).
  • What does Premium unlock? A one-time StoreKit purchase unlocks unlimited custom saved places beyond the free 20-place limit.
