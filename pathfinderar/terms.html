PathFinderAR - Terms

PathFinderAR is provided as an orientation aid only. It is not safety-certified navigation. Distances and bearings are approximate. The bundled "Split first day" sample pack lists source-verified public references and user-replaceable sample anchors; coordinates are approximate orientation anchors, not surveyed data.

Use local signs and official guidance. Check trail access, opening hours, and local conditions before you go. PathFinderAR does not certify trail legality, accessibility, or operating hours, and does not guarantee that any saved place is open or reachable.

PathFinderAR does not provide live turn-by-turn routing or real-time traffic. The compass and AR arrow show direction and approximate distance only - you walk yourself.

The app uses Apple frameworks only (SwiftUI, SwiftData, StoreKit 2, MapKit, CoreLocation, ARKit, RealityKit, AVFoundation). No third-party SDKs.

Premium is a one-time StoreKit purchase that unlocks unlimited custom saved places beyond the free 20-place limit. No subscriptions in v1.
