PathFinderAR - Privacy

PathFinderAR does not collect personal data. Saved places, area packs, and settings stay on this device.

Location is used only when you open the Compass tab or enable "My location" on the Map. Location stays on this device; it is not transmitted anywhere.

The camera is used only when you tap View arrows in AR, to render a direction arrow over the live camera view. Camera frames are not saved, shared, or sent anywhere. AR remains opt-in; the 2D compass and map have the full experience without camera access.

No analytics SDK. No advertising SDK. No account. No tracking. Purchases are handled by Apple's StoreKit. PathFinderAR is an orientation aid, not safety-certified navigation.

You can wipe all local data from Settings → Delete all local data.
