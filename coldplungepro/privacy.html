ColdPlunge Pro Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 19, 2026

ColdPlunge Pro stores every routine, session, safety acknowledgement, reminder, and setting on your device only. The app does not collect personal data.

  • No accounts, backend, analytics, ads, tracking, HealthKit, Bluetooth, or cloud sync.
  • Optional session fields such as water temperature, perceived effort, and notes stay on device.
  • CSV export is generated locally and shared only through the iOS share sheet action you choose.
  • Notification permission is requested only when you enable a routine reminder or background an active session.
  • The app does not request camera, microphone, contacts, photos, location, Health, Motion, Bluetooth, or tracking permissions.
