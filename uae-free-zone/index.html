UAE Free Zone Tax 2026

Screen UAE Free Zone revenue streams under Corporate Tax rules. Estimate whether income is qualifying at 0% or taxable at 9%, monitor your De Minimis exposure, track substance readiness, and prepare source-backed portfolio exports.

What it does

  • 0% or 9% classifier — classify each revenue stream as Qualifying or Non-Qualifying under the Qualifying Free Zone Person (QFZP) framework.
  • De Minimis monitor — track non-qualifying revenue against the lower of 5% of total revenue or AED 5 million.
  • Substance tracker — keep tabs on adequate-people-and-premises readiness.
  • Deadline reminders — registration, return, and audited-financial-statement windows.
  • Portfolio exports — export a source-backed report (Premium).
