MindFlex Support

Need help with MindFlex? Email ivan@cosmicproduction.hr with the app name, device model, iOS version, and a short description of the issue.

Common help

  • No account is required.
  • Puzzle packs are bundled with the app.
  • Progress stays on your device.
  • You can clear local progress from Settings.

Important scope note

MindFlex is a general-audience puzzle app. It is not an IQ test, therapy tool, medical product, clinical app, or cognitive assessment.
