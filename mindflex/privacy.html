MindFlex Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 20, 2026

Summary

MindFlex works without an account and without sending puzzle progress to SoftSplit or a third-party server.

Data collection

MindFlex does not collect personal data from the app in version 1.0.

Local progress

The app stores puzzle progress, streaks, missed puzzle references, sound and haptic settings, and related app state locally on the device using Apple UserDefaults. This local information is used only to run the app experience and can be cleared from Settings.

Third parties

MindFlex does not include advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, tracking SDKs, accounts, cloud sync, location, HealthKit, camera, microphone, contacts, user-generated content, or in-app purchases in version 1.0. MindFlex does not track you across apps or websites.
