CreatorFlow Support

CreatorFlow is built and supported by a single developer. We respond to support emails within a few business days.

Common questions

  • Where are my projects saved? On your device, in a SwiftData store in your app's Application Support directory. They are never uploaded.
  • Can I sync across devices? Not in v1.0. Cloud sync via iCloud is a future v1.1 candidate; we will only ship it if we can keep the privacy posture intact.
  • Can I export? Yes — plain text and markdown export are built into every project. PDF and Notion-compatible markdown bundles are planned for v1.1.
  • How do I restore a previous purchase? Settings > Premium > Restore Purchases.
  • How do I delete all data? Settings > Reset all CreatorFlow data. This is irreversible.
