Common questions

Is TaskTike for children?

No. TaskTike is parent-facing and is not listed in the Kids category. Parents manage members, chores, points, rewards, and reminders.

Does TaskTike send family data to a server?

No. v1 is local-only. There is no account, backend, cloud sync, analytics SDK, ad SDK, chat, or location tracking.

Can I use real names?

We recommend nicknames. TaskTike does not need real names, birthdays, school names, photos, contact details, or locations.

How do I delete everything?

Open Settings and choose Delete all local data. If a Parent PIN is set, the PIN gate appears first.
