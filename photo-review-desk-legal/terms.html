Photo Review Desk Terms of Use

1. What Photo Review Desk is

Photo Review Desk is a private photo and video review utility. It helps you look through your own Photos library and decide which items to delete. You make every keep / delete decision yourself.

2. What Photo Review Desk is not

Photo Review Desk is not a system cleaner. It does not free system memory, remove system caches, modify files belonging to other apps, scan for malware, or surface hidden software. It does not claim to make your phone faster, repair power health, or optimize storage outside of items you choose to delete from your Photos library.

3. User-controlled deletion

Photo Review Desk never deletes items automatically. When you confirm a delete batch, Photo Review Desk uses Apple's system Photos deletion confirmation flow. You must confirm in the system sheet before any item is removed. Deleted items go to the Photos Recently Deleted album per Apple's policy and remain there for the period Apple defines.

4. Honest labeling

Photo Review Desk labels candidates with conservative wording. Suggested duplicates are labeled as possible duplicates. Suggested blurry photos are labeled as possible blur. Estimated storage figures are estimates only and may differ from system reports.

5. No warranty

Photo Review Desk is provided as is. You are responsible for backing up your Photos library before any cleanup session. We recommend keeping iCloud Photos turned on or an alternative backup in place. The estimated storage freed is informational only.

6. Premium purchases

Premium is a one-time purchase that unlocks unlimited analysis, similar photo review, large video cleanup, possible-blur review, and screen recording review. Purchases are billed by Apple and follow the App Store terms.
