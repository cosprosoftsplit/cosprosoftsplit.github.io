SisuDaily Terms

SisuDaily is a lifestyle reflection journal. It is not a medical or mental-health tool and does not diagnose, treat, or cure any condition. For mental-health concerns, please consult a qualified professional. If you are in crisis, please contact your local emergency services or a mental-health crisis line.

SisuDaily is inspired by the Finnish idea of sisu but is not a complete representation of Finnish culture.

The premium unlock is a one-time purchase. There is no subscription.
