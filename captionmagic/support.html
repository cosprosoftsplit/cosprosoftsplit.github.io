How Caption Magic works

You pick a photo. Caption Magic uses Apple's Vision framework to detect general categories on your device. You then fill a short wizard (platform, post type, tone, niche, topic). The app matches your inputs to bundled caption templates and shows 3 to 5 suggestions you can copy, save, or share.

Common questions

  • Does Caption Magic post for me? No. You always copy or share the caption yourself.
  • Does Caption Magic upload my photo? No. Photo analysis runs on your device with Apple Vision.
  • Is this a partner of Instagram or TikTok? No. Caption Magic is not affiliated with any social platform.
  • Why are suggestions similar? Suggestions are filled from bundled templates and your inputs. The same inputs produce the same suggestions.
  • Can I add my own templates? Not in version 1. You can add brand presets to keep tone consistent.
