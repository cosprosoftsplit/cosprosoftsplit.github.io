Australia PAYG & Super Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 19, 2026

Australia PAYG & Super is a local checklist and reminder app by Cosmic Production d.o.o.

Data Collection

The app does not collect, sell, share, or transmit personal data to our servers.

Local App Data

Workspaces, employee aliases, tasks, payment reference aliases, reminder labels, source-review state, and app settings are stored locally on your device using Apple's app storage technologies.

Sensitive Data

The app is designed for alias-first records and does not ask for TFNs, BSBs, bank account numbers, salary amounts, fund member numbers, dates of birth, or government login credentials. Defensive local checks reject common sensitive-number patterns in note fields.

Notifications

If you enable reminders, the app may schedule local notifications on your device. These reminders are controlled by your device notification settings and are not sent through our servers.

Purchases, Advertising, and Tracking

The current v1 app does not include in-app purchases, advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, or tracking across apps and websites.
