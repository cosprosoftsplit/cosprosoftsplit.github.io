Common Questions

Is this an ATO app?

No. Australia PAYG & Super is not an ATO product and is not affiliated with the Australian Taxation Office, Fair Work Ombudsman, or any government agency.

Does the app calculate PAYG or super amounts?

No. It is a checklist and reminder utility. It does not calculate PAYG withholding, super guarantee, BAS, IAS, penalties, or payroll amounts.

Where is my data stored?

Your workspace records, aliases, tasks, and reminders stay on your device.

Why did reminders not appear?

Open Settings in the app and enable reminders. Also check iOS Settings to confirm notifications are allowed for Australia PAYG & Super.
