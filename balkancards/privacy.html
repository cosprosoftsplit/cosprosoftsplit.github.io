Balkan Cards Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 19, 2026

Balkan Cards is an on-device manual budgeting app. The app does not collect personal data, does not create an account, does not use analytics, and does not send budget entries to a SoftSplit server.

  • Budget plans, boxes, entries, templates, reminder choices, and entitlement display state are stored locally on the device.
  • Premium purchases are handled by Apple through StoreKit.
  • Notification permission is requested only after the user enables the check-in reminder in Settings.
  • Balkan Cards does not include third-party analytics, crash reporting, advertising, or account-linking SDKs in version 1.0.
  • Users can remove local app data from Settings by using Delete all local data, or by deleting the app from the device.
