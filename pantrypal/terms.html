PantryPal Terms of Use

PantryPal is a home pantry organizer. By using it you agree to these terms.

Not a food-safety tool

PantryPal does not determine if food is safe to eat. Best-before dates you enter are reminders only, based on what you choose to set. Always check labels and use your own judgment about whether to eat anything in your pantry. PantryPal does not detect spoilage. It does not detect allergens. It does not provide nutrition information or dietary advice. It does not provide medical advice.

On-device only

Items, events, custom zones, and bundled idea cards stay on your device. We do not run a server for your data.

Receipt reading

Receipt text recognition runs on your device via Apple's Vision framework. Extracted text is shown to you for per-line review before saving. The reading is best effort and may contain errors. Review each line carefully before saving.

Idea cards

Bundled idea cards are short prose prompts written by us. They are not recipes. They are not nutritional advice. They are not allergy advice. They are not dietary advice. Always check labels on the ingredients you are using.

Premium

Premium is a one-time purchase that removes the active item limit and unlocks custom zones. Purchases are processed by Apple's App Store. Restore Purchases re-checks your Apple ID for an existing purchase.
