PantryPal

PantryPal is a local pantry organizer for iPhone. It helps you keep an inventory, set best-before reminders, review receipt lines before saving them, and browse bundled pantry idea cards.

Privacy-first scope

PantryPal stores pantry data on your device. It does not run analytics, upload receipts to a server, use third-party advertising SDKs, or provide food-safety, nutrition, allergy, medical, or diet advice.
