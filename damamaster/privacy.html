DamaMaster Privacy Policy

DamaMaster is an offline single-device Turkish Dama trainer and pass-and-play table companion.

Data Collection

DamaMaster does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data. App Privacy in App Store Connect should be declared as Data Not Collected.

Local Storage

The app stores match history, puzzle progress, preferences, and a StoreKit entitlement cache locally on the device. None of this data leaves the device.

Permissions

DamaMaster does not request Contacts, Location, Microphone, Camera, Photos, Health, Bluetooth, Calendar, tracking, or advertising permissions.

Third Parties

DamaMaster contains no third-party SDKs, analytics, advertising IDs, telemetry, tracking domains, or network services. It uses Apple frameworks including SwiftUI, SwiftData, StoreKit 2, and Foundation.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact support@softsplit.com.
