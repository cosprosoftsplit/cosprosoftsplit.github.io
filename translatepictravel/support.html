TranslatePicTravel Support

For help with TranslatePicTravel, contact Cosmic Production d.o.o. through the support channel configured in App Store Connect.

Useful details to include: your device model, iOS version, the language pair, and whether the issue happened while scanning, importing, translating, saving to a trip, or restoring a purchase.

TranslatePicTravel stores scans and phrases locally on your device. Support cannot recover data deleted from the app.
