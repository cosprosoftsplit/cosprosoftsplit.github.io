TranslatePicTravel

TranslatePicTravel is an on-device travel text utility for menus, signs, tickets, receipts, and apartment notes.

The app uses Apple's Vision, VisionKit, Translation, SwiftData, and StoreKit frameworks. It does not use a third-party translation provider, analytics SDK, advertising SDK, account system, or cloud OCR service.

Translations are a travel aid only and are not suitable for medical, legal, immigration, emergency, or official decisions.
