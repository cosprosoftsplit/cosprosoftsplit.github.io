TranslatePicTravel Privacy Policy

Data Not Collected.

TranslatePicTravel runs OCR and translation on your device using Apple's Vision and Translation frameworks. We do not send your images, recognized text, trips, saved phrases, or analytics to any server.

Camera images are processed locally and stored in your trip library only when you tap Save to trip. You may delete trips, scans, and saved phrases from the Settings tab.

Language pack downloads are managed by iOS and are subject to Apple's privacy policy.

No account is required. No analytics SDK is bundled. No advertising SDK is bundled. No third-party translation provider is used.

Translations produced by this app are a travel aid only and are not suitable for medical, legal, immigration, emergency, or official purposes.
