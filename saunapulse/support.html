Common questions:

  • Bundled presets are conservative. Premium unlocks Classic Two Rounds and Gentle Contrast.
  • The End Now button is available at every state of an active session; tap it once to end immediately, no confirmation.
  • If you background the app during a session, we schedule a local notification for each segment transition. You can disable this from Settings.
  • Sessions log a subjective feel tag (Mild / Moderate / Hot / Very hot). No numeric temperature input. No sensor reading.
