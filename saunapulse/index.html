SaunaPulse

SaunaPulse is a private sauna interval timer and on-device journal. It helps you run simple Prep, Heat, and Cool timers, end a session immediately, and optionally save a subjective session note.

The app has no account, no analytics, no advertising, no cloud sync, no HealthKit, no Bluetooth, no sensor integration, and no third-party SDKs.

Premium unlocks the two current bundled premium routines: Classic Two Rounds and Gentle Contrast.
