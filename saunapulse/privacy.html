SaunaPulse Privacy

SaunaPulse stores every routine, session, acknowledgement, and reminder on this device only. No data leaves your phone. No analytics. No advertising. No third-party libraries.

Optional fields you enter in the session log (subjective feel tag, perceived effort, free-text note) are stored alongside the session timestamp and durations. You can delete all local data from Settings at any time.

We never request access to your camera, microphone, contacts, photos, or location. We do not integrate with HealthKit. We do not integrate with Bluetooth devices. No sensor data is read.

Notification permission is requested only when you toggle on a routine reminder in Settings, or choose to background an active session. If you decline, the timer still works.
