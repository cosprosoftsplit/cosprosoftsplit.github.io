Common Questions

Does SoundScape make music for me? No. SoundScape is a planning tool. It creates a text or markdown cue sheet that you can hand off to a video editor, DAW, or audio producer.

Why is the preview so simple? The preview is procedurally generated on this device. It is a sketch reflecting your brief. License real audio assets separately for production.

Does SoundScape track me? No. SoundScape collects no personal data and has no analytics.

Can I import audio? No. SoundScape does not import audio in this version.
