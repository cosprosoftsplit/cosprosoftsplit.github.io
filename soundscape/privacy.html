SoundScape Privacy Policy

Effective: 2026-05-18

SoundScape is a privacy-first, on-device-only soundscape planner and cue-sheet tool. Your projects, layer plans, cue timelines, and rights-checklist notes are stored locally on this device. SoundScape does not collect, store, or transmit personal data off the device.

What We Do Not Collect

  • No account. No sign-in. No email collected by SoundScape.
  • No analytics. No tracking. No advertising identifier.
  • No microphone access. No camera access. No photo library access.
  • No location. No notifications. No HealthKit. No Bluetooth.
  • No audio file uploads. SoundScape does not upload your project content anywhere.

Procedural Audio Preview

The optional procedural audio preview is synthesized at runtime on this device by Apple's AVAudioEngine. No audio is recorded, uploaded, or stored.

Purchases

Purchases are handled by Apple's StoreKit. SoundScape does not see your payment information.
