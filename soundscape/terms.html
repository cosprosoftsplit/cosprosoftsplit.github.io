SoundScape Terms of Use

Effective: 2026-05-18

SoundScape is a planning tool that helps you describe and document an audio bed for your video, podcast, meditation, or game project. It produces text and markdown cue sheets and an optional procedural audio sketch. It does not generate or transform copyrighted music.

What SoundScape Is

  • A template-based planner for audio cue sheets and layer plans.
  • A rights checklist to help you keep track of audio licenses.
  • A procedural audio sketch tool for a quick preview of the brief.

What SoundScape Is Not

  • Not a music generator. Not an audio recorder.
  • Not a copyright clearance service. You are responsible for the audio you license and publish.
  • Not affiliated with any music platform.

Procedural Audio Preview

The procedural preview is a sketch reflecting your brief. You should license real audio assets separately for production use.

SoundScape is provided "as is" without warranty of any kind. Use at your own risk.
