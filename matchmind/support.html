Frequent questions

I bought a module on another device — how do I get it here?
Open Profile > Restore purchases. The same Apple ID will restore everything.

I'm not seeing a daily scenario.
Pull-to-refresh on the Today tab, or relaunch the app. Daily content rolls over at midnight in your local timezone.

Where do my answers go?
Nowhere — they're stored on your device and never uploaded. See Privacy Policy.
