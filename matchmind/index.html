MatchMind

Dating coaching that fits in your pocket. Private by design — no accounts, no real conversations leave your phone.

What it does

MatchMind teaches the unsexy mechanics of modern dating through short lessons, practice scenarios with instant coaching feedback, and a daily 5-minute training session. Everything runs on-device.

Six focused modules:
  • Profile Basics — audit your profile, rewrite your bio
  • First Messages — better openers, real practice
  • Conversation Flow — keep momentum after the first reply
  • Asking for a Date — move from chat to date without being vague or pushy
  • Red Flags & Safety — spot the patterns early
  • Ghosting & Boundaries — handle disappearance without spiraling
