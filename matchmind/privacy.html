MatchMind — Privacy Policy

Effective: 2026-04-29

Summary

MatchMind is privacy-friendly by design. We do not require an account, do not ask you to upload real conversations, and do not store any of your dating profiles or messages.

Data we do NOT collect

  • Names, email addresses, phone numbers
  • Real dating profiles, photos, or screenshots from other apps
  • Real chat content from any dating app
  • Location data
  • Contacts

Data stored only on your device

  • Your onboarding answers and dating archetype
  • Your training progress, scores, and saved tips
  • Your purchase status (cached locally for offline use)

Third parties

The free version of MatchMind shows advertising via Google AdMob, which may collect a randomized device identifier (IDFA) for ad measurement only if you grant permission via the iOS App Tracking Transparency prompt. You can revoke this anytime in iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking. Premium users see no ads at all.

Purchases

Purchases are handled through Apple's standard in-app purchase system. We never see your payment information.
