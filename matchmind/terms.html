What MatchMind is

MatchMind is an educational self-improvement app focused on dating communication skills. It provides lessons, quizzes, and pre-baked branching scenarios. It is not a dating app, a matchmaking service, or a coaching service.

No outcome guarantee

MatchMind does not guarantee matches, dates, relationships, or specific outcomes of any kind. Dating involves two consenting adults — the only person whose behavior you can change is yourself.

Safety

If someone makes you feel unsafe, pressured, or uncomfortable: end the interaction, leave the situation, and seek support from trusted people or local authorities. MatchMind teaches you to recognize warning signs but is not a substitute for your judgment or for professional help.

Respect & consent

By using MatchMind you agree to use what you learn in service of respectful, consensual communication. The app is explicitly not designed for manipulation, coercion, or pressure. We reserve the right to terminate access for misuse.
