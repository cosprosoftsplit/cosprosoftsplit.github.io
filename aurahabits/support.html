Common Questions

Where is my data stored?

Your habits, completions, reflections, aura state, and settings stay on your device.

Why did reminders not appear?

Open AuraHabits Settings and enable reminders. Also check iOS Settings to confirm notifications are allowed for AuraHabits.

What does Premium unlock?

AuraHabits Premium is a lifetime purchase that unlocks unlimited active habits, full reflection history, and premium quest packs as they arrive.

Is AuraHabits a medical or mental-health app?

No. AuraHabits is a lifestyle habit tracker and reflection journal. It does not diagnose, treat, or cure any condition.
