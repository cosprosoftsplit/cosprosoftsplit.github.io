AuraHabits Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 19, 2026

AuraHabits is an offline-first habit tracker and reflection journal by Cosmic Production d.o.o.

Data Collection

AuraHabits does not collect, sell, share, or transmit personal data to our servers.

Local App Data

Habits, completions, reflections, aura state, settings, and local entitlement-display data are stored on your device using Apple's app storage technologies.

Notifications

If you enable reminders, AuraHabits schedules local notifications on your device. These reminders are controlled by your device notification settings and are not sent through our servers.

Purchases

AuraHabits Premium purchases are handled by Apple App Store in-app purchase. Apple processes payment information. AuraHabits only reads the purchase entitlement needed to unlock premium features.

Advertising and Tracking

AuraHabits v1 does not include advertising SDKs and does not track you across apps or websites.

Contact
