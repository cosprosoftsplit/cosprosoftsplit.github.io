How PromptVision works

Pick a template from the Templates tab. Fill the fields. The app renders a structured brief deterministically — same inputs produce the same output. Save it as a project, add new versions when you revise, and export as plain text or markdown to the system share sheet.

Common questions

  • Does PromptVision generate images? No. PromptVision is a creative brief organizer. It outputs text briefs you can take to any tool or human collaborator.
  • Does PromptVision call any third-party service? No. The whole app runs on your device with Apple frameworks only.
  • What does the Reference tab do? When you pick a photo, Apple Vision runs on your device to detect general categories and any visible text. The results are surfaced as suggested mood and style chips. Photos stay on your device.
  • Why does the safety check block render? The local linter blocks mentions of living artists, public figures, trademarked brands, copyrighted characters, and commercial-rights claims to protect you from common rights issues. Switch the safety check to Lenient in Settings to warn only.
  • Why are renders deterministic? PromptVision is not a creative writing service. The renderer substitutes your field values into bundled templates. Same fields, same brand preset, same output.
