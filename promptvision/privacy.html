PromptVision Privacy

Last updated: 2026-05-20

PromptVision is a private, on-device creative brief organizer. We do not collect personal data, run analytics, or transmit photos off your device.

What PromptVision does on your device

  • Stores brief projects, brand presets, and version history locally using Apple's SwiftData store.
  • When you pick a reference photo on the Reference tab, runs Apple's Vision framework locally to classify the photo into general categories (for example "outdoor", "food", "interior") and to read any visible text.
  • Fills the templates you pick with the words you type and shows the rendered prompt to you.
  • Lets you copy or share the rendered prompt with the system share sheet.

What PromptVision does not do

  • It does not upload your photos to any server.
  • It does not call any cloud creative-writing service, large language model, or third-party API.
  • It does not log in to any social platform on your behalf.
  • It does not post anything for you. You copy or share content yourself.
  • It does not generate images. It is a creative brief organizer, not a creator of imagery.
  • It does not track you across apps or websites.

Permissions

  • Photo Library: only requested the first time you tap Import reference photo. Used only to read the photo you pick.
  • Camera: not used or requested in version 1.

What stays on your device

Your projects, brand presets, version history, and preference state stay in this app's local SwiftData store. There is no cloud sync in version 1.

Bundled templates

Templates, brand-preset starters, and safety-checker word lists are bundled with the app and read locally. There is no remote download of content.
