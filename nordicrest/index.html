NordicRest

NordicRest is a quiet evening app for procedural soundscapes, gentle rest timers, saved routines, and local notes.

The app runs on device, uses Apple frameworks, and does not include ads, analytics, accounts, cloud sync, HealthKit, or third-party SDKs.
