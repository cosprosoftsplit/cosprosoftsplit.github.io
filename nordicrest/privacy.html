NordicRest Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 20, 2026

NordicRest is an on-device evening soundscape and rest timer app. We do not operate a backend for the app, do not create user accounts, do not include analytics, and do not include advertising SDKs.

Data Collection

We do not collect personal data from NordicRest. Routines, notes, preferences, reminder settings, and acknowledgement state are stored locally on your device.

Purchases

Premium purchases are processed by Apple through StoreKit. We do not receive your payment card information.

Notifications

If you enable the evening reminder, iOS asks for notification permission at that time. Reminder scheduling happens on your device.
