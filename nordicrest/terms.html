NordicRest Terms

Last updated: May 20, 2026

NordicRest provides procedural soundscapes, rest timers, routines, and local notes for personal use. It is not a substitute for professional guidance, and no outcome is promised.

Safe Listening

Use comfortable volume levels and stop playback if it feels uncomfortable. The in-app volume cap is provided as a convenience.

Purchases

Premium is a one-time non-consumable purchase processed by Apple. Apple handles billing, refunds, and purchase restoration under App Store terms.

Local Data

Deleting the app or using the in-app delete action can remove local routines, notes, and preferences from the device.
