FinanceFlash

FinanceFlash is a private, offline financial-literacy micro-learning app for short lessons, scenario checks, review prompts, and a searchable money-vocabulary glossary.

The app has no account, no cloud sync, no analytics, no advertising SDK, no tracking, no bank linking, no credit-score access, no portfolio import, and no market data.

Premium is a one-time lifetime unlock for the current premium topic packs: Investing Vocabulary and Money Myths.

FinanceFlash teaches general money concepts for education only. It does not provide financial, investment, tax, legal, credit, or insurance advice.
