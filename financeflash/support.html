Quick tips

  • Today loop. Open the Today tab to start the day's concept stack. Each card is short. Tap an answer; the next card queues automatically.
  • Topics tab. Browse all topic packs. Free includes Budgeting, Debt Vocabulary, and Saving. Premium unlocks Investing Vocabulary and Money Myths.
  • Review tab. Spaced-repetition-lite review of scenarios you missed. They cycle back after one day, three days, then seven days.
  • Glossary tab. Search any term across all packs. Filter by topic.
  • Progress tab. See your streak, best streak, total lessons completed, concepts in review, plus settings.

Daily concept nudge

In Progress > Daily concept nudge, toggle the reminder on and pick a time. Notification permission is asked the first time you turn it on, never at app launch. All reminders stay on this device.

Streaks

Your streak counts consecutive days you completed at least one lesson. Missed days break the streak. Best streak is preserved.

Premium

The free tier includes the Today loop, Budgeting, Debt Vocabulary, Saving, and basic glossary search. Premium is a one-time lifetime unlock for the current premium packs, Investing Vocabulary and Money Myths. To restore Premium on a new device, tap Progress > Restore Purchases.

Delete data

Progress > Delete all local data. Confirm. This removes every lesson completion, missed-scenario entry, streak day, daily-concept record, glossary entry, and the disclaimer acknowledgement. Premium unlock is preserved by design. This cannot be undone.

FAQ

  • Does FinanceFlash sync across devices? No. v1 is fully on-device. Each device tracks its own progress.
  • Does the app connect to my bank? No. There is no bank linking, no transaction import, no Plaid or open-banking integration.
  • Does the app see my credit score? No. There is no credit-report access.
  • Is FinanceFlash personal financial advice? No. FinanceFlash teaches general money concepts. For personal decisions about taxes, debt, savings, investing, retirement, or insurance, please consult a qualified professional who knows your situation.
  • Why doesn't the app ask for notification permission at launch? Permissions are requested only when you turn on the daily-nudge reminder, never up front.
