Brainrot Quiz Party

Brainrot Quiz Party is an original quiz and local pass-and-play party deck app by Cosmic Production d.o.o.

The app uses bundled fictional prompts. It has no account system, backend service, public chat, online rooms, or user-generated card publishing in v1. No analytics. No ads. No tracking.
