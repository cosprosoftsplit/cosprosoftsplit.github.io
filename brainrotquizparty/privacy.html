Brainrot Quiz Party Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 19, 2026

Brainrot Quiz Party is built as a local quiz and pass-and-play party deck app.

Data collection

The app does not collect, sell, or transmit personal data to Softsplit. It has no account system, backend service, analytics SDK, advertising SDK, tracking SDK, chat, public profiles, or cloud sync in v1.

No analytics. No ads. No tracking.

On-device data

The app stores quiz progress, streaks, missed card IDs, preview expiry, and a local entitlement cache on the device using Apple-provided storage. Pass-and-play player aliases are used locally during a round and are not sent to Softsplit.

Purchases

Premium purchases are handled by Apple through StoreKit. Apple may process payment and transaction information under Apple's own policies. The app reads Apple-provided entitlement status to unlock premium features.

Children

The app is not submitted as an Apple Kids Category app. The bundled content is designed to avoid real-person, brand, sexual, medical, harassment, gambling, and unsafe user-generated content.

Contact

For support, visit the support page linked from the app or email support@softsplit.com.
