DuoDayHabit Privacy

DuoDayHabit is a private shared-ritual tracker for two people on a single device. It does not collect, sync, or share any personal data. All information stays on your device.

  • No accounts. No sign-in. No cloud sync.
  • No analytics. No third-party SDKs.
  • No contact import. No location access. No microphone. No camera.
  • The participant names you enter during setup are local aliases. They never leave the device.
  • You can delete all local data at any time from Settings (double confirmation).

If reminders are enabled, the iOS notification system schedules them locally on your device. No reminder content is sent to any server.
