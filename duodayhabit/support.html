DuoDayHabit Support

For questions, feedback, or local-data deletion help email ivan@cosmicproduction.hr.

Common questions:

  • Where is my data? On your device only. Nothing is uploaded.
  • How do I delete everything? Settings → Delete all local data → confirm twice.
  • How do reminders work? Reminders are scheduled locally and only fire on this device.
  • Can two devices sync? Not in this version. v1 is pass-and-play on one device.
