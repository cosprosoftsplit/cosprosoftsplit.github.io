For support, contact Cosmic Production d.o.o. at ivan@cosmicproduction.hr.

For help with Property Cost Atlas, include your app version, device model, iOS version, country pack, and a short description of what happened.

Please do not send property addresses, contracts, tax records, mortgage paperwork, or other sensitive documents by email.

The app stores scenarios locally. If you want to remove local data, open Settings and use Delete all scenarios and overrides.

Property Cost Atlas cannot provide real estate, mortgage, tax, legal, lending, valuation, or investment advice.
