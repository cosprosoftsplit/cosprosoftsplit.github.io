Property Cost Atlas Terms

Effective date: May 19, 2026

Property Cost Atlas provides planning estimates based on user-entered information and dated official source links. It is not real estate, mortgage, tax, legal, lending, valuation, or investment advice.

Use Of Information

You are responsible for checking current official sources and consulting qualified local professionals before acting on any property, rental, tax, or fee decision.

No Listings Or Referrals

The app does not list properties, recommend agents, recommend brokers, recommend lenders, estimate property values, file forms, or transmit information to authorities.

Sources

Country packs include source dates and links. Source rules can change, and the app may show stale-source warnings when source checks are older than the configured policy window.
