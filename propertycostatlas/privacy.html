Property Cost Atlas Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 19, 2026

Property Cost Atlas stores property scenarios locally on your device. The app does not send scenarios, property prices, rent amounts, notes, or source choices to Softsplit or any third party.

Data Collection

The app does not collect data from this app. There is no account, analytics SDK, advertising SDK, tracking SDK, backend sync, or cloud service in v1.

On-Device Data

Saved scenarios are stored in app-local storage on your device and can be deleted from Settings.

Permissions

The app does not request camera, microphone, location, contacts, photo library, HealthKit, or tracking permission.

Contact

For support, contact Cosmic Production d.o.o. at ivan@cosmicproduction.hr or use the support page linked from the app's Settings screen.
