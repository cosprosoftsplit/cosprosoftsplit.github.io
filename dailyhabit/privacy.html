Privacy Policy

Updated: 17 May 2026

Summary

DailyHabit is a private, offline habit tracker. Your habits, check-offs, notes, and streak data are stored on this device. There is no account, no cloud sync, no analytics, and no tracking in v1.

What stays on this device

  • Habits you create (name, cadence, focus flag).
  • Check-offs and skip / snooze records.
  • Notes you attach to a habit completion.
  • Archived habits and streak repair credits.
  • Settings and notification preferences.

What we do not collect

  • No contacts, location, microphone, camera, photos, health data, or Bluetooth.
  • No third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.
  • No social ranking. No leaderboards. No friend-graph.

Notifications

DailyHabit can send local reminders for each habit you opt in to. The notification permission is requested only when you turn on a reminder, not at first launch. All reminders stay on this device.

Purchases

If you buy Premium, the purchase is processed by Apple's StoreKit. We do not receive your name, email, or payment details.

Deleting data

Settings > Delete all local data removes every habit, completion, note, archive record, streak repair credit, and entitlement cache. A second confirmation step protects against accidental taps. You can do this at any time without uninstalling.

Not a treatment tool

DailyHabit is a lifestyle habit tracker. It is not a medical or mental-health tool and does not diagnose, treat, or cure any condition. For mental-health concerns, please consult a qualified professional.
