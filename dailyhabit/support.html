Quick tips

  • Add a habit. Habits tab > tap "+" > pick a cadence (daily, every N days, selected weekdays, or N times per week).
  • Check off a habit. On the Today tab, tap the row to check off. Tap again to undo within the same day.
  • Tap-and-hold to reset. On a habit row, press and hold to access "Skip once", "Snooze one day", or "Reset for today" actions without leaving the list.
  • Focus habits. Keep up to three active habits visible on the Today board so the app stays simple and scannable.

Reminders

Open a habit's edit sheet and toggle "Reminder" to schedule a local notification. Notification permission is asked the first time you turn one on, not at app launch. All reminders stay on this device.

Streaks

Your streak counts consecutive scheduled days you completed (or were on snooze for). Skipped days break the streak. Streak repair credits (v1.1 UI) let you patch a missed day, with a monthly cap to keep streaks honest.

Premium

The free tier ships with up to 3 active habits. Premium is a one-time lifetime unlock that removes the limit and unlocks full history, richer insight history, and CSV export. To restore Premium on a new device, tap Settings > Restore purchases.

Delete data

Settings > Delete all local data. Double-confirm. This removes every habit, completion, note, archive, streak repair credit, and entitlement cache. This cannot be undone.

FAQ

  • Does DailyHabit sync across devices? No. v1 is fully on-device. Each device tracks its own habits.
  • Can I export my data? Yes. Premium users can export check-off history as a CSV from Settings > Export history.
  • Why doesn't the app ask for notification permission at launch? Permissions are requested only when you turn on a reminder — never up front.
  • Is this a mental-health or ADHD app? No. DailyHabit is a lifestyle habit tracker, not a medical or mental-health tool. For mental-health concerns, please consult a qualified professional.
