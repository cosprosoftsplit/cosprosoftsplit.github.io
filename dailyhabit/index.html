DailyHabit

A private, offline habit tracker for a few routines you want to keep visible.

What it does

  • Track up to three active focus habits for free.
  • Use local reminders only for habits you choose.
  • Review streaks, 30-day history, and simple weekly insights.
  • Unlock unlimited focus habits, full history, richer insight history, and CSV export with a lifetime Premium purchase.

Privacy

No account, no cloud sync, no analytics, no ads, and no tracking. Habit data stays on the device.
