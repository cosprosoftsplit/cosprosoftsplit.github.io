PensionPath Privacy

PensionPath stores everything you enter on this device only. We do not collect, transmit, or sync your pension information. We do not link to your bank, your pension provider, or any government system.

We do not collect analytics. We do not use third-party advertising or tracking. We do not require an account. We do not request access to your contacts, location, microphone, camera, photos, health data, calendar, or Bluetooth.

The application uses an in-device database for the entries, contribution periods, documents, questions, reminders, and acknowledgement records you create. You can erase all of this from Settings, "Delete all local data".

Notification permission is requested only when you set the first reminder. If you decline, your reminder data is still stored locally; only the system notification is suppressed.

PensionPath is education only. This is not financial, investment, tax, legal, or pension advice. Verify all information with primary sources and qualified professionals.
