QuickNoteAR

Private quick notes for rooms, desks, errands, and the small things you want to remember in context.

What It Does

  • Capture quick notes and organize them by room.
  • Attach photo references from a user-initiated picker.
  • Set one-time local reminders.
  • Optionally place a note card on a detected surface in AR.

Privacy Posture

QuickNoteAR runs on device. There are no accounts, no cloud sync, no analytics SDKs, no advertising SDKs, and no third-party SDKs in v1.

Camera access is requested only when you tap Place in AR. Photo selection starts only when you tap Attach Photo. Notification access is requested only when you create a reminder.

AR placement is approximate and lives on this device only.
