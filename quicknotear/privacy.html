QuickNoteAR Privacy

QuickNoteAR runs entirely on your device. Notes, rooms, reminders, AR placement metadata, and photo references are stored locally in the app's container. There is no cloud sync, no shared anchors, no analytics, no third-party SDKs.

QuickNoteAR does not collect personal data.

Camera access is requested only when you tap "Place in AR". No camera frames are saved or shared.

Photo Library access is requested only when you tap "Attach Photo". Selected images stay on this device unless you export or share them.

Notification access is requested only when you create your first reminder.
