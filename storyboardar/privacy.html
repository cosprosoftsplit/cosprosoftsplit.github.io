StoryBoardAR Privacy

StoryBoardAR runs entirely on your device. Projects, scenes, settings, and entitlement status are stored locally in the app container. There is no cloud sync, no analytics, no tracking, no ads, and no third-party services in v1.

StoryBoardAR does not collect personal data.

Camera access is requested only when you tap Camera Guide. The preview shows framing grids only. No video is recorded or saved, no photos are captured, and camera images are not uploaded.

No notifications, photo library, location, contacts, microphone, or HealthKit permissions are requested.
