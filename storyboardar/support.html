StoryBoardAR Support

StoryBoardAR is an offline-first shot planner. If something looks stuck, force-quit and relaunch the app, then reopen your project.

  • Use Restore Purchases in Settings to recover Premium.
  • Use the Settings app to manage camera permission.
  • Use Clear All Projects in StoryBoardAR Settings only if you want to remove local project data.

The camera guide is preview-only and does not record or save video.
