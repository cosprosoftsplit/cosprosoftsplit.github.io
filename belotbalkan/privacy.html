BelotBalkan Privacy Policy

Data Not Collected.

BelotBalkan does not collect any personal data. Scorekeeper history, drill progress, and settings stay on your device.

The app has no analytics SDK, telemetry, cloud sync, third-party network requests, advertising identifiers, tracking SDKs, Game Center, online multiplayer, or chat.

Delete all local data in Settings removes local scorekeeper history and drill progress.

Effective date: 2026-05-20.
