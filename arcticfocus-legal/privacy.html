ArcticFocus Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-20

ArcticFocus stores app data locally on your device. There are no accounts, no developer servers, no analytics, no ads, and no third-party SDKs.

We do not collect personal data. We do not track you across apps or websites. We do not sell or share information.

Notification permission is requested only when you enable a routine reminder or when you choose to background an active session.

You can delete local sessions, routines, and reminders from Settings at any time.
