HolidayCal 2026 Terms

Last updated: 19 May 2026

HolidayCal 2026 is provided as a planning calendar and countdown utility. It is not an official government calendar and is not affiliated with any government body.

Planning only

The app does not provide legal, HR, payroll, employment, school-calendar, or religious advice. Before booking travel, time off, events, or services, verify dates with the relevant official source, employer, school, or local authority.

Bundled content

Holiday packs are bundled with the app and may become stale as laws, proclamations, or source pages change. The Sources screen shows the review date and source status for each pack.

Reminders

Local reminders are best-effort device notifications. Delivery can depend on device settings, Focus modes, and operating-system behavior.

Premium and ads

The free tier may show banner and interstitial ads. HolidayCal Premium is a yearly auto-renewable subscription that removes ads while active. Prices and renewal terms are shown by the App Store before purchase, and subscriptions can be managed or cancelled in App Store settings.

Croatia and the United Kingdom are the only user-facing v1 country packs.
