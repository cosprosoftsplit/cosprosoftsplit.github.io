HolidayCal 2026 Privacy Policy

Last updated: 19 May 2026

HolidayCal 2026 is a local holiday calendar, countdown, and long-weekend planning app with bundled, source-tracked event packs.

Data

The current app stores selected packs, favorites, reminder offsets, private notes, and display preferences on the device using UserDefaults. This data is not sent to SoftSplit or a third-party server.

The app also stores a local display cache of Premium status. Purchases and subscription entitlement checks are handled by Apple's App Store systems.

Permissions

Notification permission is requested only after you choose to set a reminder. Free users may also see Apple's tracking permission prompt for advertising. The app does not request location, contacts, calendar, camera, or microphone permission.

Third parties

The free tier uses Google Mobile Ads and Google's User Messaging Platform to show ads and manage consent. Premium removes in-app ads. The app does not include a live calendar-data backend.

Advertising partners may process device identifiers, coarse location inferred from network information, advertising data, product interaction data, and diagnostic data for advertising, analytics, measurement, consent, fraud prevention, and SDK performance purposes, subject to consent and platform settings. App Store purchase processing is handled by Apple.

Ad choices

Ad consent and privacy options are available in the app under Settings > Ads & Privacy when Google UMP provides options for the user's region. Users can also manage Apple tracking permission in iOS Settings.

Sources

Holiday data is bundled and versioned. Source links are shown in the app so users can verify dates locally before relying on them.
