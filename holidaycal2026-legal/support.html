HolidayCal 2026 Support

Last updated: 19 May 2026

For help with HolidayCal 2026, email support@softsplit.com with the app name, device model, iOS version, selected holiday pack, and a short description of the issue.

Official sources

Use the in-app Sources screen to open the official references used by each bundled pack. For UK bank holidays, see gov.uk/bank-holidays.

Premium and ads

HolidayCal Premium removes ads while the yearly subscription is active. Use Settings > Premium to purchase or restore. If ads still appear after purchase, restart the app and use Restore Purchases.

Publishing status

This support page is prepared for handoff and should be rechecked before App Store publication.
