BE E-Invoice Readiness

BE E-Invoice Readiness is a private preparation checklist for Belgian B2B structured e-invoice readiness.

The app does not send invoices, validate VAT numbers, connect to Peppol access points or accounting systems, file VAT returns, or provide tax, legal, accounting, VAT, or compliance advice.

The app is independent and is not affiliated with the Belgian government, FPS Finance, the European Commission, or any Peppol authority.
