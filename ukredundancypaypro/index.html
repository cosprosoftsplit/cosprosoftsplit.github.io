UK Redundancy Pay Pro

UK Redundancy Pay Pro is an independent, on-device planning estimator for statutory redundancy pay in Great Britain.

Important scope note

The app is not legal, tax, employment, or financial advice. It is not a guarantee of entitlement and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or approved by GOV.UK, Acas, the Insolvency Service, or any UK government body.
