UK Redundancy Pay Pro Privacy Policy

Last updated: 19 May 2026

Overview

UK Redundancy Pay Pro is a private, on-device planning estimator for statutory redundancy pay in Great Britain. It does not require an account and does not send your scenarios to a server.

Data stored on your device

The app may store saved scenarios, checklist items, and local premium cache state in Apple UserDefaults on your device. This data stays local unless you choose to share a planning summary using iOS sharing features.

Data collection

The app does not collect, sell, or transmit personal data to Softsplit. It has no analytics SDK, no advertising SDK, no tracking SDK, no account system, and no backend service.

In-app purchases

Premium purchases are handled by Apple through StoreKit. Apple may process payment and transaction information under Apple's own policies. The app reads Apple-provided entitlement status to unlock premium features.

Official sources

The app links to GOV.UK and Acas pages for source checking. Opening external links may be handled by Safari or Apple's system browser, which are outside this app.

Delete local data

You can delete saved scenarios, checklist items, and local premium cache state from the app's More screen. Deleting the local cache does not revoke an Apple purchase; purchases can be restored.
