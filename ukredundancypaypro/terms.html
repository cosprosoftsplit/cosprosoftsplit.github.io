UK Redundancy Pay Pro Terms

Last updated: 19 May 2026

Purpose

UK Redundancy Pay Pro provides planning estimates for statutory redundancy pay in Great Britain using source values checked against GOV.UK and Acas guidance.

No legal advice

The app is not legal, tax, employment, or financial advice. It is not a guarantee of entitlement and does not replace GOV.UK, Acas, your contract, your employer's written calculation, or a qualified adviser.

Scope

The app covers Great Britain only: England, Wales, and Scotland. Northern Ireland statutory rates are out of scope.

Excluded topics

The app does not calculate enhanced contractual redundancy, notice pay, holiday pay, pension, benefits, tax, PILON, settlement agreements, insolvency claims, tribunal strategy, or unfair-dismissal issues.

No government affiliation

The app is independent and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or approved by GOV.UK, Acas, the Insolvency Service, or any UK government body.

Premium purchase

Premium is a lifetime non-consumable in-app purchase handled by Apple. It unlocks unlimited saved scenarios, scenario comparison, and shareable planning summaries.
