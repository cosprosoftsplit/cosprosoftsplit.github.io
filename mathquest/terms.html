MathQuest Terms

Last updated: 2026-05-18

MathQuest provides general arithmetic practice for children ages 6-9. It is not a school, tutoring service, curriculum guarantee, assessment service, or substitute for a parent, guardian, or teacher.

Parent responsibility

A parent or guardian should decide whether the app is appropriate for a child and supervise usage as needed.

No social features

The app has no chat, messaging, public leaderboard, public profile, user-generated content, or account system.

Premium unlock

The full unlock is a lifetime non-consumable in-app purchase handled by Apple StoreKit.
