MathQuest Support

Need help with MathQuest? Email support@cosmicproduction.hr with your iOS version, app version, and a short description of the issue.

Common questions

Does MathQuest collect child data?

No. There are no child names, child ages, photos, accounts, analytics, or tracking.

Does MathQuest use AI tutoring?

No. Practice problems are generated locally by deterministic app logic.

Does MathQuest show ads?

No. MathQuest has no ads and no third-party advertising SDK.
