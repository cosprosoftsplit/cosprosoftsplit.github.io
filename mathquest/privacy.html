MathQuest Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-18

MathQuest is an ad-free arithmetic practice app for children. The app does not collect personal data, analytics, tracking identifiers, photos, microphone input, location, contacts, school information, or account information.

Local storage

Progress, badges, settings, and parent-gate state remain on device using Apple local storage APIs. There is no backend account, no cloud sync, and no public profile.

No advertising or third-party SDKs

MathQuest does not include third-party advertising, analytics, crash-reporting, or social SDKs.

StoreKit

The full unlock is handled by Apple's StoreKit. MathQuest does not receive or store payment credentials.

Contact

For privacy questions, email support@cosmicproduction.hr.
