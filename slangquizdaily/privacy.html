Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-20.

Slang Quiz Daily is built around a "Data Not Collected" posture. No analytics, no tracking, no advertising identifiers in this version. All quiz progress, missed terms, and preferences are stored locally on your device using Apple's standard UserDefaults + SwiftData APIs.

StoreKit purchases are handled by Apple. We do not see your payment information.

You can delete all local data at any time from Settings → "Delete all local data" (double confirmation required).
