DiwaliLight

DiwaliLight is a respectful planning companion for Diwali week. It helps with countdowns, preparation checklists, greeting drafts, decor ideas, and source notes for bundled date records.

Dates, names, and customs vary by region, community, and family practice. DiwaliLight is not a religious authority, does not provide ritual instruction, and keeps regional date records clearly marked for reviewer sign-off before submission.

Privacy

The app stores preferences, checklist items, greeting drafts, and optional private reflections locally on device. It has no ads, tracking, analytics, account system, or third-party SDKs in v1.
