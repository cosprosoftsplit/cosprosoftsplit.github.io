Common questions

Why is the date different from my family's calendar? Diwali is a 5-day lunisolar festival. Regional, community, and family practice can shift naming and timing. Use the Today tab "Adjust today" CTA or Calendar tab "Adjust this day" CTA to set the date for your celebration.

Which region should I pick? The 8 region variants are best-effort defaults. Pick the closest one and adjust the preparation checklist if needed.

Why are all dates marked "Review needed"? v1 ships date claims as drafts pending a native Hindu / Sikh / Jain / Tamil-Hindu reviewer pass. We will remove the chip once the reviewer pass is recorded.

How do I clear my data? Settings > Local data > Clear all local data.
