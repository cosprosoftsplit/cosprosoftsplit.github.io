RamadanPulse Support

My calculated times do not match my community's times.

Open Settings → Times and methods, and adjust the calculation method (six standard options are bundled) and the global offset. For per-day adjustments, open Calendar, tap a day, and apply a suhoor or iftar adjustment.

How do I turn reminders on?

Open the Reminders tab. Toggle on each kind you want (suhoor, iftar, personal preparation, charity, reading, Eid morning). The very first toggle prompts iOS for notification permission.

How do I export my reflections?

Reflection export is not included in v1. Your entries stay local on this device unless you remove them with Delete all local data.

How do I delete everything?

Settings → "Delete all local data" (double confirm).
