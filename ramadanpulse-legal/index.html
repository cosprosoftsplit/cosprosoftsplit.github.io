RamadanPulse

RamadanPulse is a private Ramadan planning companion for calculated time windows, reminders, household checklists, source notes, and local-only reflections.

Times and dates are calculated estimates. RamadanPulse is not a religious authority, does not provide fatwa or medical guidance, and does not claim to replace local community schedules or moon-sighting guidance.

Privacy

The app stores settings, reminders, checklist state, and reflections locally on device. It has no ads, tracking, analytics, account system, cloud sync, third-party SDKs, or device-location permission in v1.
