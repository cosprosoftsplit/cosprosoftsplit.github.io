RamadanPulse Privacy

Effective date: 2026-05-17

RamadanPulse is a private planning and reminder utility. It does not collect, transmit, or sell personal data.

What stays on your device

  • Your selected city, calculation method, and offsets.
  • Your daily Ramadan calendar adjustments and private notes.
  • Your private reflection entries (gratitude / intention / reading progress / personal note).
  • Your household checklist completions and grocery notes.
  • Your reminder preferences.

What we do not do

  • No accounts, no sign-in, no cloud sync (v1).
  • No analytics SDKs. No advertising SDKs.
  • No third-party SDK ships in the app binary (verified by an automated absence test).
  • No tracking domains. No App Tracking Transparency prompt is shown.

Permissions

  • Notifications — requested only when you toggle a reminder kind on for the first time. Denied? The app shows a small dashboard badge instead of repeating the prompt.
  • Location — device-location permission is not requested in v1. The City Picker stores your selected city from the bundled list; manual mode works with no city selection at all.

Deleting your data

Settings → "Delete all local data" removes every reflection, checklist state, and adjustment from this device after a double-confirm. Uninstalling the app removes the SwiftData store entirely.
