RamadanPulse Terms

Effective date: 2026-05-17

What this app is

RamadanPulse is a private planning and reminder utility for the month of Ramadan. Times are calculated estimates based on user-selected settings and can be manually adjusted. The app does not provide religious rulings, medical advice, or official community schedules. Reflections and checklists are stored locally unless you choose to export them.

Calculated estimates

Suhoor, iftar, and prayer times are computed deterministically from your selected city / calculation method / madhab / high-latitude rule / global offset. They are not authoritative. Local moon sighting and community practice may differ; please follow your local community guidance where applicable.

What this app is not

  • Not a religious authority. No fatwa. No theological ruling.
  • Not a mosque or community schedule. Manual time entry is supported for that case.
  • Not a medical or fasting health advisor. Consult a doctor for fasting health questions, especially if you have diabetes, are pregnant, or are caring for children.
  • Not a worship-performance tracker. No streaks. No leaderboards. No public comparisons.

Acceptable use

This app is for personal household planning and personal reflection. Use it in ways consistent with your local laws and your conscience.

The one-time premium purchase unlocks unlimited household checklists, full reflection history, and the premium reflection prompt library in this app version. It does not promise unshipped roadmap items, official schedules, or religious guidance.
