SpeechPerfect Practice Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-20.

What we collect

SpeechPerfect Practice does not collect personal information. The app keeps your practice session log, your self-rating responses, and any audio you choose to record entirely on this device. We do not have a server, an account system, or a way to identify you.

Audio recordings

Recording is optional. The microphone is only used after you tap Record and grant permission. Audio files are saved to this app's private container. They are never uploaded, never shared with us, never analyzed for any purpose. You can delete a single recording with one tap, or delete every recording in one action from Settings.

What we do not do

This app is a self-guided practice utility. It does not provide a healthcare service. It does not analyze your voice for any objective quality score. It does not infer your health, identity, age, gender, or any protected characteristic from your voice. It does not generate any guidance using machine-learned audio models on a remote server.

Third-party services

SpeechPerfect Practice is not affiliated with any external audio analysis service, any external voice-recognition cloud service, any ad network, any analytics provider, or any external healthcare provider. The app does not send any data to outside companies.

On-device speech recognition

Version 1.0 does not expose a read-along screen and does not request Speech permission. If a future on-device read-along view is added, recognized text will be shown to you only, and will not be stored, scored, or shared.

Children

SpeechPerfect Practice is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
