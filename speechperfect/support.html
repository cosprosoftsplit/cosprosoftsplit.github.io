SpeechPerfect Practice Support

Quick answers

Does this app analyze my voice? No. The app does not perform any objective scoring or grading on your audio.

Do recordings leave my device? No. Recordings stay in the app's private container. You can delete them at any time.

What if I deny the microphone permission? Recording is unavailable. Every other feature (scripts, timers, self-rating, progress, premium) still works.

What does Premium unlock? In version 1.0, Premium unlocks every bundled practice drill beyond the free starter limit.
