SpeechPerfect Practice

SpeechPerfect Practice is a private self-guided speaking-practice app for short daily drills, optional local recording, local playback, and self-rated progress.

The app has no account, no cloud sync, no analytics, no advertising, no tracking, and no third-party SDKs in version 1.0. Optional recordings stay in the app's private container unless the user chooses to share them.

Premium is a one-time lifetime unlock for every bundled version 1 practice drill beyond the free starter limit.
