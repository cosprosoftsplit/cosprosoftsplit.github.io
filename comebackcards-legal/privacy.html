Privacy

Comeback Cards is an offline app. It does not collect, transmit, or share personal data. Your favorites and preferences are stored only on your device using Apple frameworks (SwiftData + UserDefaults).

The app does not:

  • Create accounts or require login.
  • Analyze your real messages or contacts.
  • Use cloud generation or third-party generation services.
  • Share decks with other users.
  • Track you across apps or sites.
  • Include third-party analytics or advertising SDKs in version 1.0.

StoreKit is used only when you tap Restore Purchases or buy the lifetime unlock. Apple receives the transaction; we do not.
