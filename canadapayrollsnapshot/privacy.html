Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 19, 2026

Canada Payroll Snapshot is designed as a local-only utility. User-entered snapshots, optional notes, and checklist status are stored on the device using Apple UserDefaults.

Data Collection

The app does not collect data from your device for the developer or any third party. It has no account system, backend, analytics SDK, advertising SDK, tracking SDK, cloud sync, or crash-reporting SDK in v1.

Sensitive Data

The app is alias-first. You should use generic labels such as "Sample employee." The app's local guard rejects SIN-shaped and bank-account-shaped digit runs, but you should still avoid entering confidential payroll records or identity numbers.

Permissions

The app does not request Camera, Photo Library, Location, Contacts, Microphone, Calendar, Reminders, Notifications, Health, Motion, Bluetooth, or Tracking permissions.

Contact

Use the App Store support channel for questions about this policy.
