Support

For Canada Payroll Snapshot support, contact Cosmic Production through the App Store support channel or email the publisher account used for your purchase/download.

Before You Write

Please include your iOS version, app version, and a generic description of the issue. Do not send a SIN, bank number, legal name, employer account number, paystub, or confidential payroll record.

What The App Does

Canada Payroll Snapshot is a local source-pointer and checklist app. It does not calculate payroll, file payroll, generate official forms, or provide tax, payroll, legal, employment, or accounting advice.
