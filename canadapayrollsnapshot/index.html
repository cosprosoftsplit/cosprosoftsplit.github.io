Canada Payroll Snapshot

Canada Payroll Snapshot is a local, source-linked 2026 payroll reference for Federal + Ontario and Federal + Quebec planning snapshots.

It shows CRA and Revenu Quebec rate cards, source links, local checklist items, and planning-summary export. It does not calculate net pay, tax, CPP/QPP, EI/QPIP, paystubs, T4 slips, or filings.

Privacy

The app stores user-entered snapshots and checklist status locally on the device. It has no account, analytics SDK, advertising SDK, tracking, cloud sync, or government affiliation.

Important

This app is not a CRA, Revenu Quebec, Service Canada, or Ontario WSIB product and does not provide tax, payroll, legal, employment, or accounting advice.
