Common Questions

Does PetCarePro provide veterinary advice?

No. PetCarePro is a record keeper and reminder tool. It does not diagnose illness, recommend treatment, prescribe medication, interpret doses, or replace advice from your veterinarian.

Where is my data stored?

Your app data is stored locally on your device. Version 1 has no account system, backend, analytics, advertising, or cloud sync.

How do I delete my data?

Open Settings in the app and choose "Delete all local pet data."

How do I restore purchases?

Open Settings and tap "Restore purchases." Purchases are handled by Apple through StoreKit.
