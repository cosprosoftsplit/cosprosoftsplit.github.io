Terms of Use

PetCarePro is provided as an offline-first record keeper, reminder dashboard, and sitter-handoff helper for pet owners.

The app does not diagnose illness, recommend treatment, prescribe medication, interpret dosage, provide emergency triage, or replace advice from a licensed veterinarian.

Reminder templates are starter examples only. Users are responsible for entering dates, instructions, doses, and care decisions based on their own veterinarian's advice.

Premium purchases, if enabled, are handled by Apple through StoreKit. Use Settings > Restore purchases to re-check entitlements.
