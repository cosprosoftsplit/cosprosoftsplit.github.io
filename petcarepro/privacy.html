Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 19, 2026

PetCarePro stores the information you enter locally on your device, including pet profiles, reminders, care events, vaccine records, medication notes, vet visits, weight entries, document-reference rows, sitter notes, settings, and premium entitlement state.

The app does not use analytics, advertising, tracking, a remote backend, cloud sync, third-party SDKs, or a developer-operated API. The developer does not collect data from this app.

PetCarePro may request notification permission only after you create a reminder or explicitly enable reminders in Settings. Notifications are scheduled locally on your device.

PetCarePro does not request location, contacts, camera, microphone, or photo-library permission in version 1.

If premium products are enabled, purchases are handled by Apple through StoreKit. PetCarePro checks Apple-provided entitlement state on device and does not send purchase data to a developer server.

You can delete local app data from Settings by choosing "Delete all local pet data." Removing the app from your device also removes local app data according to iOS behavior.
