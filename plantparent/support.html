Common Questions

Where is my data stored?

Your plant profiles, schedules, notes, and care history stay on your device.

Why did reminders not appear?

Open PlantParent Settings and enable reminders. Also check iOS Settings to confirm notifications are allowed for PlantParent.

What does Premium unlock?

PlantParent Premium unlocks unlimited active plants and CSV care history export.

Can I use PlantParent without an account?

Yes. PlantParent does not require login or account creation.
