PlantParent Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 19, 2026

PlantParent is an offline-first houseplant care reminder app by Cosmic Production d.o.o.

Data Collection

PlantParent does not collect, sell, share, or transmit personal data to our servers.

Local App Data

Plant profiles, care schedules, notes, and care history are stored locally on your device using Apple's app storage technologies. Deleting the app or using the in-app delete option removes local app data from that device.

Photos

The current v1 app does not include plant photo capture or photo library attachment features.

Notifications

If you enable reminders, PlantParent schedules local notifications on your device. These reminders are controlled by your device notification settings and are not sent through our servers.

Purchases

PlantParent Premium purchases are handled by Apple App Store in-app purchase. Apple processes payment information. PlantParent only reads the purchase entitlement needed to unlock premium features.

Advertising and Tracking

PlantParent v1 does not include advertising SDKs and does not track you across apps or websites.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact ivan@cosmicproduction.hr.
