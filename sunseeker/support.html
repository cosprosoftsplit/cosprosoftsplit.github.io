FAQ

Why are my sun times slightly different from another app? Sun times are an astronomical approximation. SunSeeker rounds to the minute. Differences of one to two minutes are normal.

Why no weather? v1 ships sun times only. Weather is planned for a future update via Apple's WeatherKit.

Why no location prompt at launch? SunSeeker asks for location only when you tap Use my current location. You can also add cities by name without granting location.
