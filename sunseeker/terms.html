What SunSeeker does

SunSeeker calculates sunrise, sunset, golden hour, solar noon, civil twilight, and daylight duration from latitude, longitude, and the current date using a native port of the public-domain NOAA Solar Position Algorithm. v1 does not include live weather, UV index, or air quality data.

What SunSeeker does NOT do

SunSeeker does not diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any condition. It is a planning utility only and makes no health, wellness, or treatment claims of any kind. It does not guarantee weather conditions or travel safety.
