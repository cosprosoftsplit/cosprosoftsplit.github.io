StoryTime Support

How StoryTime works

You pick a story pack, an age band, a length, a tone, and an optional theme. You can also type a friendly character nickname, a favorite color, a friendly place, and a helpful object. The app assembles a short prose story from a curated template on this device. You then read it aloud at bedtime, or save it for later.

Common questions

  • Does StoryTime use generation services? No. Stories are filled from curated original templates and the words you type. Nothing is sent to a server.
  • Can my child use the builder? The builder is intended for parents. The Parent Gate protects purchases, settings, and delete actions from accidental child taps.
  • Why is one pack free? The Bedtime Calm pack is free so you can decide whether StoryTime is a good fit before unlocking the full library.
  • Will my saved stories sync to other devices? Not in version 1. Saved stories live only on this device.
  • Can I read the same story to several children? Yes. Same inputs always produce the same story, so it is easy to read it again.
