StoryTime Terms of Use

What you get

StoryTime is a parent-controlled bedtime story builder. The parent picks a pack and a few simple options, the app assembles a short prose story from a curated template on this device, and the parent can read it aloud or save it locally. Stories are template-based and deterministic. The same inputs always produce the same story.

  • You are responsible for reading any story before sharing it with a child.
  • Do not use StoryTime to make medical, therapeutic, developmental, or sleep treatment claims.
  • Do not type a child's full name, birthday, school, address, phone, or any sensitive personal information into any field.

Premium

Premium is a one-time lifetime unlock. It removes the free pack limit, unlocks all curated story packs, and allows unlimited local saves.
