StoryTime Privacy

StoryTime is a private, on-device, parent-controlled bedtime story builder. We do not collect personal data, run analytics, or transmit any input off your device.

What StoryTime does on your device

  • Loads a small library of curated, parent-friendly story templates from this app's bundle.
  • Lets the parent pick a pack, an age band, a length, a tone, and an optional theme.
  • Lets the parent optionally type a friendly character nickname, a favorite color, a friendly place, and a helpful object.
  • Fills the chosen template with the parent's inputs on this device. The result is a short prose story for the parent to read aloud.
  • Lets the parent save the rendered story locally in this app's storage.

What StoryTime does not do

  • It does not use any live language model, cloud generation service, or third-party generation API.
  • It does not record, store, or upload any child voice, child photo, child full name, birthday, school, address, or location.
  • It does not let a child type prompts directly into any generation engine. The story builder is parent-only.
  • It does not include chat, social network, public sharing, or user-generated content publishing.
  • It does not include ads in version 1.
  • It does not track you across apps or websites.
  • It is not affiliated with any media company or character brand.

What stays on this device

Saved stories, your parent gate preference (math, PIN hash, or biometric opt-in), your reading mode, and your font scale stay in this app's local store. There is no cloud sync in version 1.

Permissions

  • StoryTime requests no permissions at launch.
  • Face ID or Touch ID is requested only if the parent has chosen biometric as the Parent Gate method and taps a gated action. The system prompt fires only then.

Stories are template-based

All stories are assembled locally from curated original templates plus the optional inputs the parent typed. There is no model-based generation and no cloud language service.

StoryTime is not a medical, therapeutic, or developmental app

StoryTime is family entertainment for the parent and the child to enjoy together at bedtime. It does not provide medical, therapeutic, developmental, or sleep treatment advice. Please use it as a small bedtime warmth, not as a treatment.
