Privacy

VisaCheck is privacy-first. All data you enter is stored on your device only.

  • We do not run a backend server, do not sync your data, and do not transmit it anywhere.
  • We do not use analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, crash reporters, or any third-party telemetry.
  • We do not collect personal information beyond what you type into the app on this device.
  • We never ask for, store, or display passport numbers. The Notes fields refuse passport-number-shape input.

What we store on your device

  • Traveler profile nickname (e.g. "Me" or "Spouse"), citizenship country codes you enter.
  • Travel document metadata: nickname, country code, type, expiry date, plain notes (no passport numbers).
  • Stay windows you log (country code + entry / exit dates).
  • Trip folders + checklist items + custom questions you create.
  • Local app-lock preference (off by default).

Notifications

VisaCheck only requests notification permission when you enable a reminder on a specific document. Reminders are local notifications scheduled by iOS itself.

Optional biometrics

If you enable App Lock in Settings, VisaCheck uses LocalAuthentication (Face ID / Touch ID) to unlock the app. The biometric check happens on your device; no biometric data leaves your device.

Deleting your data

Settings > Clear all local data permanently deletes every profile, document, stay window, trip folder, checklist item, and question. Uninstalling the app deletes everything too.

Children

VisaCheck is rated 4+ but is intended for adults managing their own travel paperwork. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
