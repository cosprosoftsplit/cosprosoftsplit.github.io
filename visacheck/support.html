Common questions

Why doesn't VisaCheck tell me if I need a visa? Visa rules depend on your nationality, your purpose of travel, your destination's current policy, and dozens of edge cases. We are not an immigration authority and we don't pretend to be. The Sources tab links you to the official government pages so you can read the rule yourself.

Why can't I save the passport number? We don't store passport numbers. The Notes field refuses passport-number-shape input on purpose. We don't want a leaked phone to leak your passport number too.

Reminders are not firing. Open Settings > Notifications and make sure VisaCheck has permission. Permission is requested the first time you enable a reminder on a document.

I want to delete everything. Settings > Clear all local data wipes every profile, document, stay window, trip, and checklist item permanently. Uninstalling the app removes everything as well.

Restore Purchases. Settings > Restore Purchases re-checks your premium entitlement against the App Store.
