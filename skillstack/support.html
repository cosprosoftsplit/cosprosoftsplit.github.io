Quick tips

  • Today loop. Open the Today tab to start the day's lesson stack. Each lesson is short. Tap an answer; the next concept queues automatically.
  • Library tab. Browse all packs. Free includes Workplace Basics and Learning How To Learn. Premium unlocks Code Basics Preview, Money Basics Vocabulary Preview, and Culture and History Starter Preview.
  • Learn tab. Dive deeper on a specific lesson or concept outside the daily stack.
  • Review tab. Spaced-repetition-lite review of concepts you missed. Missed questions surface again on a short ladder.
  • Progress tab. See your streak, best streak, total lessons completed, concepts in review, plus settings.

Daily lesson nudge

In Progress > Daily lesson nudge, toggle the reminder on and pick a time. Notification permission is asked the first time you turn it on, never at app launch. All reminders stay on this device.

Streaks

Your streak counts consecutive days you completed at least one lesson. Missed days break the streak. Best streak is preserved.

Premium

The free tier includes the Today loop, Workplace Basics, and Learning How To Learn. Premium is a one-time lifetime unlock for the bundled SkillStack 1.0 premium preview packs: Code Basics Preview, Money Basics Vocabulary Preview, and Culture and History Starter Preview. To restore Premium on a new device, tap Progress > Restore Purchases.

Delete data

Progress > Delete all local data. Confirm. This removes every pack install, lesson completion, missed-question entry, streak day, and daily-stack record. Premium unlock is preserved by design. This cannot be undone.

FAQ

  • Does SkillStack sync across devices? No. v1 is fully on-device. Each device tracks its own progress.
  • Can I export my data? Not in v1.
  • Why doesn't the app ask for notification permission at launch? Permissions are requested only when you turn on the daily-nudge reminder, never up front.
  • Is SkillStack a certification, exam, or job preparation tool? No. SkillStack teaches concepts and vocabulary. It does not prepare you for any certification, exam, hiring process, or school curriculum.
  • Is the Money Basics Vocabulary Preview financial advice? No. It teaches vocabulary only. For financial decisions, please consult a qualified professional.
