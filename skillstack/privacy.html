Privacy Policy

Updated: 18 May 2026

Summary

SkillStack is a private, offline micro-learning app. Lessons you complete, missed-question entries, streak days, and pack-install state all live on this device. There is no account, no cloud sync, no analytics, and no tracking in v1.

What stays on this device

  • Pack installs and progress per pack.
  • Lesson completion records.
  • Missed-question entries (for spaced-repetition-lite review).
  • Streak days and best-streak counter.
  • Daily stack (today's lesson mix) state.
  • Settings and reminder preferences (nickname, daily-nudge time).

What we do not collect

  • No contacts, location, microphone, camera, photos, health data, or Bluetooth.
  • No third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.
  • No leaderboards, no friend graph, no social ranking.
  • No age, school, employer, parent email, or date-of-birth fields (enforced by tests).

Notifications

SkillStack can send a single optional daily lesson reminder at a time you choose. The notification permission is requested only when you turn the reminder on in Progress > Daily lesson nudge, not at first launch. All reminders stay on this device.

Purchases

If you buy Premium, the purchase is processed by Apple's StoreKit. We do not receive your name, email, or payment details.

Deleting data

Progress > Delete all local data removes every pack install, lesson completion, missed-question entry, streak day, daily-stack record, and the locally cached nickname. Premium entitlement is preserved so you do not have to repurchase. A confirmation step protects against accidental taps. You can do this at any time without uninstalling.

Not certification, not job preparation

SkillStack teaches concepts and vocabulary. It does not prepare you for any certification exam, job interview, school curriculum, or hiring process. We provide no certificates, no exam scores, and no employment guarantees.

Not financial advice

The Money Basics Vocabulary Preview teaches vocabulary only. It is not investment, tax, or financial planning advice. For financial decisions, please consult a qualified professional.
