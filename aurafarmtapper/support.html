Aura Farm Support

Need help? Have feedback? Email support@cosmicproduction.hr and include your iOS version + Aura Farm version (Settings → About).

Common questions

  • How do I restore my premium purchase? Settings → Premium → Restore Purchases.
  • How do I reset my progress? Settings → Reset progress. This is destructive and cannot be undone.
  • Is there a leaderboard or multiplayer? No. Aura Farm is a single-player offline game in v1.0.
  • Does Aura Farm read my aura or character profile? No. Aura Farm is a casual idle tapper game. "Aura crops" and "Glow" are gameplay-only mechanics, not character-profile / well-being / metaphysical claims.
