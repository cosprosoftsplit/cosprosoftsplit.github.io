Aura Farm: Tap & Grow

Aura Farm is an original offline idle tapper game with collectible crop cosmetics, deterministic rewards, and one optional lifetime Premium unlock.

"Aura" and "Glow" are gameplay-only terms. Aura Farm does not measure, read, predict, or assign any real aura, mood, health, personality, or user trait.
