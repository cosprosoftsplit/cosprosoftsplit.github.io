Aura Farm Privacy Notice

Aura Farm is an original casual idle tapper game that runs entirely on this device. Your Glow balance, upgrade levels, daily streak, and unlocked crop cosmetics stay on this device.

Data we do not collect

  • We do not run any analytics SDK.
  • We do not send your gameplay data to any server.
  • We do not use third-party advertising SDKs in version 1.0. If a future update enables ads, this notice will be updated and Apple's ATT prompt + Google UMP consent will be shown before any ad request.
  • We do not collect contacts, location, microphone, photos, calendar, health, or background-refresh data.
  • We do not require an account. There is no chat, no leaderboard, no user-generated content, and no online multiplayer.

What "Aura" means here

"Aura Farm", "aura crops", and "Glow" are gameplay-only terms — they describe the in-game currency and collectible cosmetics. Aura Farm does NOT measure, read, or assign any individual aura, vibe, energy, character profile, mood, or psychological condition. It is not a well-being, character-profiling, metaphysical, astrology, or aura-reading app.

In-app purchase

Aura Farm offers one optional non-consumable in-app purchase ("Aura Farm Premium — Lifetime") that unlocks premium cosmetic crops and extends the offline-earnings cap. There are no consumable currency packs, no loot boxes, no gacha, and no real-money gambling. The purchase is processed by Apple StoreKit. We do not receive your payment details.

Delete your data

Settings → Reset progress erases every local Glow balance, upgrade level, and crop cosmetic. Deleting the app from your device also removes any local data.

Children

Aura Farm is a general-audience casual game, not directed at children under 13, and not submitted to the App Store Kids category.
