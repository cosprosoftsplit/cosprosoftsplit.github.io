Common questions

Is this a DVLA or GOV.UK app?

No. V5C UK Drive Pack is independent and unaffiliated with UK Government services.

Does the app verify my vehicle status?

No. It organises reminders and checklists only. Check official sources for MOT, tax, insurance, and legal requirements.

Does the app store V5C reference numbers?

No. V5C document reference numbers are not requested.
