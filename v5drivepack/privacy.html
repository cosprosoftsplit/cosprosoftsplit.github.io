V5C UK Drive Pack Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-21

V5C UK Drive Pack is a local-first vehicle paperwork organiser. It does not connect to DVLA, DVSA, GOV.UK, insurers, banks, or any backend service.

Data collection

The app does not collect analytics, tracking identifiers, account data, location data, contact data, payment account data, or official vehicle-record data from government services.

Local storage

Vehicle reminders, checklist state, and notes are stored locally on your device. PDF checklist exports are generated only when you choose to share them.

StoreKit

Premium unlocks are handled by Apple's StoreKit. V5C UK Drive Pack does not receive or store payment credentials.

Contact

For privacy questions, email support@cosmicproduction.hr.
