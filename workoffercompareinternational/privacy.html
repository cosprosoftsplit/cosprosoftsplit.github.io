Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 19, 2026

Offer Compare is designed as a local-only iOS app. It does not collect personal data, create accounts, run analytics, track users, show ads, or send saved offer details to a server.

Data Stored On Device

The app may store saved offer profiles, employer aliases, salary inputs, relocation worksheet entries, notes, and manual FX reference rates in local app storage. You can delete this local data from Settings.

Network Use

The app does not call a backend, payroll provider, live FX service, job board, or analytics provider. If you tap an official source link or support/legal link, your browser opens that external website.

Advice Disclaimer

Offer Compare is not payroll, tax, immigration, legal, employment, relocation, or financial advice. It does not recommend one offer over another.
