SoftSplit Privacy

This page is a general privacy fallback for SoftSplit apps.

Many SoftSplit apps store calculations locally on the device and do not send user-entered values to a backend. Some apps may also use third-party services such as Apple for purchases or ad providers for the free experience.

Privacy practices can vary by app. If you need the exact policy for a specific app, use the legal link in that app's current App Store listing or contact support.
