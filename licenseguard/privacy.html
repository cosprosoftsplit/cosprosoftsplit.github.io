Privacy

License Guard is a personal pilot document reminder utility. It runs entirely on your device.

What we collect

Nothing. We do not run analytics, telemetry, or crash reporting. We do not have a server. There is no account.

Where your data lives

Every credential, reminder rule, renewal record, and entitlement cache is stored locally on your device using Apple SwiftData. Nothing leaves your device.

Notifications

Reminder notifications are scheduled locally via Apple's UserNotifications framework. They do not transit any server.

What this app is not

License Guard does not verify your license, rating, medical, training status, or legal authority to operate. It does not communicate with any aviation authority. It does not provide legal, regulatory, safety, or medical advice. Always confirm your status directly with the issuing authority, your examiner, your employer, or a qualified instructor.
