License Guard

A private pilot document reminder utility for dates you enter yourself.

What it does

  • Tracks pilot document dates locally on your device.
  • Schedules opt-in local reminder offsets.
  • Opens official aviation source links in Safari.
  • Exports a plain-text renewal checklist through the system share sheet.

License Guard does not verify credentials, calculate operating authority, fetch regulator data, or provide legal, regulatory, safety, or medical advice.
