Support

License Guard runs entirely on your device. Common questions:

Reminders are not firing

Make sure you have enabled at least one reminder offset on a credential AND granted notification permission when prompted. You can re-check status in Settings.

I want a fresh start

Settings includes a destructive "Delete all local data" option that erases every credential, reminder, renewal record, and entitlement cache from this device.

Contact

For product feedback please email the address listed on the App Store page. This app is a personal reminder utility; we cannot verify license status, advise on aviation regulation, or speak for any authority.
