HistorySnap

HistorySnap is an offline-first history and culture micro-learning app built around compact source-noted cards. It is designed for private, general-audience learning with no account, ads, analytics, cloud sync, or third-party SDKs.

What is included

  • Two free starter packs: World History Basics and Balkan History Starter.
  • A daily snap loop, chronological timeline browsing, and local saved snaps.
  • Source notes, date/place tags, and uncertainty markers where historical precision matters.
  • Lifetime Premium for unlimited saved snaps.

Content posture

HistorySnap is general-reader learning content. It is not a government, museum, school, legal, religious, or academic authority, and it is not a curriculum product. Short summaries are source-noted and written for context, not citation-grade research.
