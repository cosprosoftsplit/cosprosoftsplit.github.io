StaySmartSEA — Privacy

Data Not Collected.

StaySmartSEA runs entirely on this device. We do not send saved tips, notes, or any analytics to any server.

Tip content is bundled inside the app; no live fetch happens during use.

When you tap Open Source on a tip, your device opens the cited URL in Safari outside this app. Once that happens, the visited page operates under its own privacy terms.

No account is required. No analytics SDK is bundled. No third-party SDK is used. No camera, photo, location, contacts, calendar, microphone, or notification permission is requested.

Use Delete all local data in Settings to erase every saved tip and checked pre-trip item from this device.

StaySmartSEA is not legal, immigration, medical, visa, or insurance information. Verify with official sources before traveling.
