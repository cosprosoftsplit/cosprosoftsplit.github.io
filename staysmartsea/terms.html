StaySmartSEA — Terms

StaySmartSEA is a private offline travel tips utility for Southeast Asia. By using it you agree to the following.

Informational only. Tip content summarizes publicly cited travel advisories and is not legal, immigration, medical, visa, insurance, or financial information.

Verify before traveling. Conditions change. Always confirm the latest guidance with the official source linked in each tip and with the relevant embassy or qualified professional before relying on any information.

No live data. The app does not fetch live status, prices, or availability. The bundled tips have a verified-on date; tips older than 180 days surface a "may have changed" warning chip.

No marketplace. StaySmartSEA does not list rentals, hotels, transportation, or services for booking. The app does not collect commission and does not endorse any third-party brand.

Source attribution. Some tip summaries cite GOV.UK Foreign Travel Advice. Contains public sector information licensed under the Open Government Licence v3.0. GOV.UK and other cited providers do not endorse StaySmartSEA.

Local data only. Saved tips and checked pre-trip items stay on your device. Use Delete all local data to wipe them.
