StaySmartSEA — Support

StaySmartSEA is a private offline travel tips utility for Southeast Asia. Common questions and answers.

Why does the app not show live prices, availability, or weather?

StaySmartSEA does not make live network calls. Tip content ships with each app update. Tip sources open in Safari outside the app for live verification.

Why is one of my tips marked as stale?

Tips show a stale-source warning when their verified-on date is older than 180 days. Open the source link to check the latest advisory.

How do I get the full tip library?

Tap Unlock StaySmartSEA in the Settings tab. The premium unlock is a one-time purchase.

How do I remove my data?

Open Settings, scroll to Local data, and tap Delete all local data. This wipes every saved tip and checked pre-trip item from the device.
